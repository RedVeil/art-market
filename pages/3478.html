Additional Information

The collection was exhibited in the Kaiser-Wilhelm-Museum in Krefeld for a long period of time. The exhibition included key works from the Arte Povera, Minimalism and Pop Art movements by artists such as Lucio Fontana, Piero Manzoni, Mario Merz, Joseph Cornell, Lee Bontecou, Louise Nevelson, Claes Oldenburg, Tom Wesselmann, Christo, Richard Serra, Sol LeWitt, Fred Sandback, Richard Tuttle, Hanne Darboven, Joseph Kosuth. 21 lots from the collection were auctioned off for 96 million in mid-May in New York in 2008, fetching double of the estimate. Amongst them was the American Nude No.48 by Tom Wesselmann, which fetched 10.7 million dollars, a world record price.
