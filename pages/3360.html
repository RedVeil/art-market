Additional Information

Bay Publishing manages a bibliographic file and an artistic and historical vanguard international documentary with a particular emphasis on Spain, Latin America and Cantabria.
He has been the curator of the exhibitions organized by the Museum of Fine Arts of Santander: 'Julio Maruri'. Santander, 2003-2004, and 'Acknowledgements' (Collection Miguel Logroño).
He is the editor of the magazine "Art and part" from the number 100 onwards.
Lafuente is part of the jury of the International Contemporary Art and Collecting Award.
