Additional Information

Mrs. Hoff received the "Conservation Award 2009" for her work on the a property in Henningsvær, Nordland, Norway.
She opened up KaviarFactory in the Nordland of Norway to show part of her and her husband's collection, but also bring international artist to Norway.
The Hoff collection is one of the most important collections of contemporary art in the Nordic countries.
Venke Hoff collects art with her husband Rolf Hoff.
