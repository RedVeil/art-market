Additional Information

Her work experience includes photo-journalism in many African countries; audience research, television and radio production, special campaigns, marketing, writing and capacity building.
Sandra Obiago's work experience includes working in Switzerland as a Technical Director at Limelight Studios, a Reporter/Producer at the European Business Channel, and managing the communications network in Sub-Saharan Africa for the World Wide Fund for Nature (WWF).
She is co-organiser of the Nigerian The Collectors’ Series, a new Nigerian art initiative that promores Nigerian art and culture.
She organizes talks and venues about art and culture.
