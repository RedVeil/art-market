Additional Information

Brian O'Doherty is an Irish sculptor, conceptual artist, author, art critic and installation artist.
He is a pioneer in the development of Conceptual Art.
He is married to art historian Barbara Novak since 1960.
He is the author of 'Inside the White Cube: The Ideology of the Gallery Space', that was first published by O’Doherty as three separate essays in Artforum in 1976.
In 2012, he exhibited at Thomas Fischer Gallery in Berlin, Germany.
