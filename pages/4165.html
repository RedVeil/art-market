Additional Information

In 2006, Ronald S. Lauder bought Gustav Klimt's "Portrait of Adele Bloch-Bauer I" for $135 Million at Christie's—at that time, the highest price ever paid for a painting.
Lauder is an advocate and an instrumental player in the restitution movement for art stolen/lost during the Nazi era.
He is reputed to own the world’s largest private collection of medieval and Renaissance armor.
