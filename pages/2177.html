Additional Information

Although Judith and Richard Greer collect together, there also appears to be a separate 'Richard Greer Collection'.
He gives talks and advice on collecting and the contemporary art-market, such as at the Buy Art Fair, Manchester (2010).
Richard Greer is one of a group of experts for the 'School for Creative Startups', an online course which seeks to provide the business know-how and advice that creative people need to build sustainable startups.
When collecting art, he often buys works from artists he knows, as he enjoys building relationships with artists.
