Additional Information

Broad donated $23.2 million to help finance the Broad Art Center at UCLA in 2000.
Eli and Edythe Broad pledged $26 million to Michigan State University to finance the construction of the Eli and Edythe Broad Art Museum. They gave an addition donation of $2 milllion in 2010.
In 2013 Eli and Edythe Broad won $250,000 Simon Prize for Philanthropic Leadership and donated the award to the High School for the Visual and Performing Arts on Grand Avenue in downtown Los Angeles.
The Eli and Edythe Broad Foundation donated $60 million to LACMA to establish the Broad Contemporary Art Museum and purchase art in 2003.
