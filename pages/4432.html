Additional Information

He is relatively knew to collecting and asked the collector and art consultant Matthieu Gauvin for advice. He is especially attracted to the works of Contemporary Montreal painters.
He looks for a certain aesthetic, emotion, and depth in a work, rather than for it being by a certain artist. If an artwork touches him, he will take that as his starting point. Paintings are for him words which speak to him.
