Additional Information

He has one of the largest collections of Damien Hirst and Tracey Emin works.
Tim built up his collection of modern and contemporary British Artists in the mid 1990s before prices rose substantially. He met artists and gallerists through his job as a designer and gallery technician, saying,"I've met countless people who have said that they would certainly start a collection if they had the disposable income? You have to understand that most people who actually collect usually have a bank balance that is minus rather than plus. The most important thing is to collect art that you love." (The Manchester Contemporary 2012 Fair Programme)
