Additional Information

Besides collecting art, he also collects jewelry, coins, and vases as well as armour such as helmets, breastplates, backplates and swords.
He is rather critical of collectors who own rare significant works to themselves. He believes that those collectors should at least make the rare works accessible to museums.
He often visited museums in London and Paris.
His collection at the Mougins Museum of Classical Art consists of 700 works that are within the span of 5000 years.
His collection is one of the best private collection of classical art.
His museum, Musée d'Art Classiquede Mougins, contains his artworks from antiquities to contemporary art.
His passion for collecting art began with Victorian coins at the age of 7 as well as campaign medals from the First and Second World Wars
