Additional Information

Zhang Zhiyong attended the Second Annual Meeting of Chinese Contemporary Art Collectors in 2010 and exhibited his collection of Zhou Chunya and Guo Jin.
Zhang Zhiyong established the "Blue Roof Art Center" in Chengdu in 2011, which is an art community with artist studios and collector residences.
