Additional Information

The Neu-Ulm Museum's opening exhibition "Events of the Self" was chosen by Chris Dercon, director of Tate Modern, as one of the 10 best exhibitions of 2010 for Artforum Magazine.
Walther owns 70 artworks by the South African artist David Goldblatt.
Walther spearheaded the establishment the Triennial of Photography and Video in 2003 by the International Center of Photography.
In 2016, Walther received the International Center of Photography's Infinity Trustee Award.
He started collecting African photographs during a trip to Africa organized by the International Center of Photography (ICP) which led to the exhibition Snap Judgements in 2006
