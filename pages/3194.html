Additional Information

He has built up a noteworthy contemporary design collection with pieces by Zaha Hadid, Ron Arad, Campana Brothers, Tom Dixon, Ettore Sottsass, Dale Chihuly.
He is Chairman of the publishing company Mediamorfosis Ltd, Chairman of the board of PROTEAS Press Ltd., which are the largest, state-of-the-art printers in Cyprus, Chairman of the company H&A Paper Ltd, the main importer and distributor of paper and newsprint.
He registered CYCO - The Cyprus Contemporary Art Museum – a private, non profit organization, aiming to build bridges with museums in Europe and large art fairs abroad, as well as to support young artists, mainly from Cyprus.
He studied in Germany. In 1988 he moved back to Cyprus and joined the Phileleftheros Newspaper, Cyprus’ leading daily that was founded in 1955 by his grandfather Nicos Chr. Pattichis.
In the past few years, works from his collection have been exhibited as part of international events and exhibitions at major museums and galleries throughout the world.
Mr. Pattichis is the founder and publisher of more then 18 other publications. He publishes different weekly and monthly magazines on art, architecture, interior and design, recipes and gourmet, lifestyle, health, and travelling.
Mr. Pattichis organises exhibitions with his collection and publishes catalogues that accompany them.
Mr. Pattichis owns one of the leading contemporary art galleries in Cyprus, Omikron Gallery in Nicosia, Cyprus (www.omikrongallery.com).
