Additional Information

Lu Xun's farther is also a collector for traditional Chinese paintings and antiques.
Lu is heavily involved in his and his father's private museum, Sifang Art Museum. He commissions architectural works and permanent artworks around the architectural campus as well as builds the collection for the museum.
