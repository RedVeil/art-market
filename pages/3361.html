Additional Information

He is considered to be one of the leading collectors of European contemporary art in Greece.
He owns a yacht named 'Guilty', that was painted by Jeff Koons.
In 1985 he bought his first artwork for $ 2700. It was Jeff Koons' 'One Ball Total Equilibrium Tank'.
In 2010, Jeff Koons curated an exhibition in New York with works from the Dakis Joannou Collection.
In 2012, Mr. Joannou was ranked 36 in ArtReview's guide to the 100 most powerful figures in contemporary art.
Jeff Koons and Dakis Joannou are close friends, since the first time they met. Mr. Joannou has the largest collection of Jeff Koons artworks worldwide.
Mr. Joannou has said of his passion for collecting: 'Collecting is, for me, an adventure, a set of different - lived - experiences, a constant flow of meeting, talking, listening, looking. It is an act of understanding and participating.'
Mr. Joannou is the founder of Deste Foundation, center for contemporary arts, located in Athens. DESTE is a nonprofit foundation that organises exhibitions and supports publications that explore the relationship between contemporary art and contemporary culture.
