Additional Information

In 2002 one third of his collection were bought by the foundation "Preußischer Kulturbesitz" for six million euros. 236 artworks were split between two museums and the last third third was donated by Marzona to the foundation.
In the Berlin Kunstbibliothek is the archive of the Marzona collection, which contains many thousands of books and periodicals, posters and invitations, recordings, films, photographs, and letters.
Marzona has collected thousands of invitations, exhibition catalogs, and posters with unusual designs that earned to be regarded as realizing artistic ideas.
