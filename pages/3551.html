Additional Information

He houses his collection of Impressionist and Modern paintings on his yacht the Joanna Alexandra. Other works in his collection, including works by Picasso, Chagall and Miro, are displayed in his yacht, the Aviva, and in his Lyford Cay bungalow.
Listed as 308th richest person on Forbes’ List of billionaires (2013), his art collection has an estimated worth of $1 billion.
