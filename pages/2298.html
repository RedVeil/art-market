Additional Information

Frequently lends works from his collection to exhibitions in prestigious museums and galleries, a tactic he says also increases a work's value.
He paid £70,000 (in instalments across a one year period) for a brightly coloured abstract by Patrick Heron. When he split from his wife, he had to sell it at Sotheby's, where it realised £96,000.
His collection is composed almost exclusively of work by contemporary UK artists who have studied at the Royal College of Art.
Mark Turner is the largest single collector of work by Gordon Cheung and Liam Spencer, with over 50 works by the latter.
