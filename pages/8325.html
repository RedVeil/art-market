Additional Information

Maezawa paid a record-breaking $110.5 million for Jean-Michel Basquiat's 1982 painting "Untitled," at the Sotheby's Contemporary Art Evening Auction on May 18, 2017.
In late 2019, Maezawa announced he plans to sell a 30% stake in his company, Zozo to Yahoo Japan and resign as CEO. He cited making more time to train for his panned 2023 #dearmoon voyage around the moon.
Maezawa sold Andy Warhol's "Flowers" (1964) and Ed Ruscha’s "BONES IN MOTION" (2007) at auction in mid-2019.
In 2018, in partnership with SpaceX, Maezawa revealed his plans to lead an art project titled #dearMoon on a mission to fly around the moon. He will invite several artists to join him and the trip is expected to take place in 2023.
