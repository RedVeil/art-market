Additional Information

Beth Rudin DeWoody collects Iran do Espírito Santo and Dan Fischer in depth, and would like to collect as many as work from Marilyn Minter
Beth Rudin DeWoody is the daughter of New York real-estate magnate Lewis Rudin. She was married to artist Jim DeWoody (they have since divorced) and hence involved in the art scene in SoHo just as it started.
