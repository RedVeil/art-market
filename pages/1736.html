Additional Information

In 2013, Leonardo DiCaprio sat as a portrait subject for artist Elizabeth Peyton.
DiCaprio also collects vintage movie posters from the golden era of Hollywood as well as predatory dinosaur fossils.
DiCaprio hosts an annual charity auction, often including major bluechip artists like Jeff Koons, Damien Hirst, Marc Quinn, James Turrell, and Urs Fischer, in support of the Leonardo DiCaprio Foundation.
In 2013, DiCaprio orchestrated a charity auction at Christie’s entitled “The 11th Hour Sale,” to benefit environmental causes, raising a grand total of $38.8 million and setting 13 world auction records for works of art by the following artists: Carol Bove, Joe Bradley, Dan Colen, Mark Grotjahn, Sergej Jensen, Bharti Kher, Robert Longo, Adam McEwen, Raymond Pettibon, Elizabeth Peyton, Rob Pruitt, Sterling Ruby and Mark Ryden.
