Additional Information

Fabio Roversi Monaco has been honoured lot of times so far: he is honorary professor at University of La Plata and at University of Maribor. He has been awarded with Laurea Honoris Causa by Brown University of Providence, from University Complutense of Madrid, from University Panthèon-Sorbonne of Paris, from Johns Hopkins University of Baltimore, from Soka University of Tokio, from uniiversidad Externado de Colombia, from University of San Petersbourg, from University de Barcelona, from University of Cordoba, from Pontificia Universidad Cattolica of Belo Horizonte, from University of Salta (Argentina), from Université de Montréal, from University of Denver, from University Victoria of Melbourne, dall’Università Cattolica dell’Uruguay, dall’Università di Trieste, dall’Università di Maribor (Slovenia), dall’Università Statale di Samarcanda; from University G. D’Annunzio of Chieti and Pescara (Italy) Furthermore, he has received the prestigious title of "Cavaliere di Gran Croce" of Italian Republic, the title of ’“Ordem de Sant’Iago de Espada” of Portugal Republic; He has been conferred the Lègion d’Honneur by the President of French Republic; the "Croce di Grand’Ufficiale" of Ordine almerito Melitense; the "Gran Croce of Alfonso X il Savio" by the king of Spain; He received the title of Paul Harris Fellow by Rotary International.
In 2013 Mr Roversi has inaugurated the new museum of the city Genius Bononiae- Musei nella città. The Museum is hosted in different buildings spread in the city of Bologna.
Mr Roversi Monaco is the president of Fondazione Carisbon an institution that promotes the economical and cultural development of the city of Bologna. The Fondazione Carisbo is also sponsor of Furla Art Priza.
