Additional Information

In Hong Kong's China Club, guests can view all the artwork selected by Johnson Chang. (Not Johnson's own collection.)
Johnson Chang is considered as one of the most important promoters for Chinese contemporary art.
Most of Johnson Chang's art collection is stored in several warehouses in Hong Kong. However Johnson refuses to discuss any details of his collection.
