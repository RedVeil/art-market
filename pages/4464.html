Additional Information

He also looks at the amount of time and details the artists have put into the paintings. He also considers, when purchasing, the title of the art piece because he does not want to buy literal translations of the pieces.
He began to decorate his house with works of art since the mid 1970s.
He dedicated a room for Cesar Legaspi who is his favourite artist of all time.
He did lots of experimentation as well as 'heartbreaking experiences' in the development of his art collection.
He gathers information from galleries, friends or acquaintances who want to sell their collections.
He prefers not to collect paintings of which the materials are difficult to maintain such as watercolour, oil on paper.
He tends to collect from younger artists rather than big successful ones.
His taste was developed through trying various genres, going to art galleries, reading art books as well as talking to people.
