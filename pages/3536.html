Additional Information

Frank Cohen hopes his daughter and son, who now work for the Gagosian Gallery in London and New York, will keep the Dairy Art Center going after him.
Frank Cohen plans to sell 143 works by mostly young artists from his collection, through Pierre Berge auctions in 2013.
Frank Cohen started collecting Modern British art in the 1970s and then gradually expended to Young British Artists (YBAs), American and German art of the 1980s and 1990s and Japanese, Chinese and Indian art.
Frank Cohen is the founder of the Dairy Art Centre which is closed at the moment.
