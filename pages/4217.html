Additional Information

He is one of Denmark's largest and boldest art collectors.
His first piece of art was an aquarelle by the Danish artist Sven Dalsgaard, one of his eye motifs.
His interest in art is a hobby, and all of the works are, in his own words, "acquired through the desire that arises when I encounter art that attempts to say something to me".
The collection is primarily based on intuitive purchases, many of which are continuously shown at exhibitions world wide.
