Additional Information

Besides collecting art, she also collects antiques.
One of her earliest sculptures is from second century BCE to second century CE.
She also owns pottery pieces from 6-9th centuries as well as Indian sculptures from 6-7th centuries and a 16th-century figure from a Japanese temple.
She defines herself as a collector of fine arts and antiques.
She owns quite a few pieces from Japan, China, and Vietnam where the couple got exposed to the Asian art during their travel there.
