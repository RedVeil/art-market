Additional Information

Cramer also established the Douglas S. Cramer Foundation with two buildings and five different exhibition spaces on his 420-acre ranch, called La Quinta Norte, in the Santa Ynez Valley.
Cramer is routinely ranked by ARTnews as one of the top 200 collectors worldwide and his generosity has earned him the 2011 Cincinnati Art Award.
He is an art collector and television executive.
He is founder and former President of the Board of the Los Angeles Museum of Contemporary Art.
His collected artworks have been shown at some of the leading art museums in the United States, and have been auctioned at Sotheby's and Christie's (1997, 2012).
In 1997, Mr. Cramer sold 22 contemporary sculptures at Christie's in New York, for a total of $2.9 million, with proceeds of the sale going to the Douglas S. Cramer Foundation.
In 2008, he Cramer sold Man-Crazy Nurse #2 by Richard Prince for $7.4 million.
Mr. Cramer is most famous for producing such Paramount Television shows such as Mission: Impossible on CBS, Mannix on CBS, The Brady Bunch on ABC and The Odd Couple on ABC.
Mr. Cramer is one of America's leading collectors of contemporary art.
