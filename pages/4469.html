Additional Information

She collects artworks, kilims and embroideries.
She has established the Foundation for Fine Arts and Cultural Heritage, KUSAV Foundation, a charitable trust. The principal aim of the KUSAV Foundation is to preserve Turkey’s artistic and cultural heritage by actively promoting national and worldwide interest in the country’s traditions.
