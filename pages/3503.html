Additional Information

After her husband's death Mrs. Ferguson sold a great amount of her collection at auctions.
In 2008, Noeleen donated four works by Irish artist Basil Blackshaw to IMMA in memory of Vincent Ferguson.
Mrs. Ferguson's husband Vincent Ferguson died in 2007 at the age of 75. Both share a passion for art collecting.
Vincent and Noellen Ferguson owned the Hendriks Gallery on St Stephen's Green, Dublin.
