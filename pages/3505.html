Additional Information

Dermot Desmond founded NCB Stockbrokers in 1981, which eventually became Ireland's largest independent brokerage. He sold NCB in 1994 for $39 million, and started his own private equity firm, International Investment & Underwriting.
He is a keen supporter of the arts. He created an online hub and networking platform for culture and the performing arts called Cultural Odyssey to spread Irish arts and culture worldwide. (http://irish.intuition.com/).
He is an Irish businessman and financier.
He is considered to be an ecclectic art collector.
