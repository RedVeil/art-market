Additional Information

In 1999 Mr. Diamantides and his wife Maria Diamantides founded the Public Welfare Foundation "Propondis", that aims to support education, scientific research and the arts (www.propondis.gr). The Propondis Foundation offers scholarships for postgraduate and doctoral studies, both in Greece and abroad, to scientists and artists.
In 2006, Mr. Diamantides bought the painting "????? ???? ??????? ??? ??? ????????" (Still Life Before the Acropolis) by Greek artist Konstantinos Parthenis for € 674.517 at a Sotheby's Greek Sale, in 2007, he bought "??????? ??? ??????" (The Virgin and Child) by the same artist for € 950.875. Supported by the professional opinion of art historians, he sued Sotheby’s and one of its specialists over the two allegedly fake paintings in 2012.
In June 2012 Sotheby's and one of its specialists were found guilty of selling fake paintings to Mr. Diamantides.
Mr. Diamantis Diamantides is a shipping tycoon who is also a frequent buyer of Greek art.
