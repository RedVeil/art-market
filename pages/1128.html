Additional Information

Dasha Zhukova`s actual first name is "Darya".
In 2013 Dasha Zhukova and Roman Abramovich purchased a collection of works by Ilya Kabakov for a reported $60 million.
In October 2015, Dasha Zhukova gave $1 million to the Massachusetts Institute of Technology to endow a brand new visiting artist program at the MIT Center for Art, Science and Technology.
In 2011, GARAGE Magazine was launched by Ms Zhukova, who serves as the editor-in-chief.
Dasha Zhukova supported a $400 million renovation of a romantic “art island” called New Holland in St. Petersburg.
