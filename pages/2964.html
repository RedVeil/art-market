Additional Information

For him, collecting is about experiencing the works of art on a daily basis. It is not about financial investment, although he does recognize that it would be a welcome secondary benefit.
Most of the artworks he collects are chosen with his house in mind, as he enjoys seeing how a work of art can complete or enhance a room. He also enjoys seeing how visitors react to a piece.
Over time, his taste and choices have become bolder and more controversial. Whereas he began collecting works which were primarily abstract or focussed on colour, he has now moved on to urban and graffiti art.
The first artwork which he purchased was by a struggling photographer friend who was experimenting with painting.
