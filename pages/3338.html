Additional Information

George Embiricos died in late 2011, his wife and sons inherited his fortune, including his art collection. In 2011 and 2012 they enlisted Sotheby’s to sell a group of artworks. A painting by Cézanne showing two card players brought them $ 250 million.
He is the son of Greek shipping magnate George Embiricos.
The George Embiricos Collection was one of the 15 most important collection of modern and contemporary art worldwide.
