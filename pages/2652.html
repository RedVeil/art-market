Additional Information

John Axelrod collected American prints, European Art Deco objects, American modern decorative arts from 1920 to 1950, Memphis furnishings, African-American painting, and Latin American art in the past. He sold parts of his collection and donated most of it to museums. Now he collects street art and graffiti.
