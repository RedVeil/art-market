Additional Information

In 2012, Laura Rapp and Jay Smith were responsible for bringing an important piece of video art by Christian Marclay titled The Clock to the National Gallery of Canada (NGC) in Ottawa.
Laura Rapp is involved in sponsoring a series of lectures called "The Rapp Lectures in Contemporary Art" which is held in the Art Gallery of Ontario.
