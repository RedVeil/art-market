Additional Information

Jean Albou published a book called "Un fou dans l'art" in France , his autobiography which tells also about his mental illness.
Jean Albou sold 120 pieces of his collection on january 2008, the 29th, in Paris at Artcurial, for about 6 M €.
Jean Albou sold a part of his collection in order to create a new space for arts in La Croix Valmer, in the south of France.
