Additional Information

According to Korean art magazine "Art price", she has the most powerful influence and is the biggest art collector in Korea.
She bought Roy Lichtenstein's "Happy tears" for USD 7.15 million from Christie's New York auction in 2002. She bought Willem de Kooning's "Untitled ?" for 30 billion won (USD 34,805,600,000) from Seomi Gallery in Korea. She suffered discredit for purchasing expensive artworks with secret fund of Samsung in 2007.
She is the wife of Samsung CEO Lee Kun-hee. She is a director of Leeum which the biggest private museum of art in Korea.
