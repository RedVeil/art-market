Additional Information

Wang Cheng-hua attended the 2010 Second Annual Meeting of Chinese Contemporary Art Collectors and exhibited his collection of Zhou Chunya.
Wang Cheng-hua exhibits some of his collection at TAD Associates office, located in Shanghai. He also invites external people to visit his collection during the regular art events held in the company.
