Additional Information

He sees his collecting tastes as differing when it comes to furniture and art: with furniture, he turns towards the past; whereas with art, he is turned towards the future, with a focus on Contemporary art.
Patrick and Laurence Seguin are especially known as collectors of Jean Prouvé's work, owning the world’s largest collection of his structures, all stored in warehouses in Nancy.
They own the designer Jean Royère's archives: thousands of drawings, blueprints, and photos, of which many were published in a catalogue raisonné.
