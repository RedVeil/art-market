Additional Information

He has given speeches on "Chinese Contemporary Art" at many art events like Art Basel, Miart and Hong Kong Sotheby. Guan Yi is known for his Chinese contemporary collection, and he begins to collect western contemporary artwork since 2010.
His art collection was referred to by Westerners as “the best way to understand Chinese contemporary art.”
In January 2014, Guan Yi donated 37 works of contemporary Chinese art to the M+ museum.
