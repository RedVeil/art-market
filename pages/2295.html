Additional Information

Maria and Malek Sukkar believe that they collect in order to better understand themselves, the world which surrounds them and the events which take place and affect them.
Maria and Malek Sukkar see the process of collecting as something very personal, which gathers momentum as one discovers first one, then another work which one likes.
Maria Sukkar is part of the Tate's MENA and Photography Acquisitions Committees and the British Museum's Middle East Acquisition Committee.
