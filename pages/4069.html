Additional Information

266 of the works that had not been hung on the walls of their house for many years were auctioned by Bonhams (selling 86.5%) on 24th March 2013 at Sydney's Museum of Contemporary Art.
According to Colin's Wikipedia entry: "Since 2000 131 paintings have been donated to the Newcastle, Geelong, Benalla and Gold Coast regional galleries, the Museum and Art Gallery of the Northern Territory, the National Gallery of Victoria, the Art Gallery of NSW and the Museum of Contemporary Art in Sydney. 53 of these have been works by 41 different Indigenous artists and 78 have been by 35 different non-Indigenous artists."
Elizabeth's collection was formed with her late husband, Colin, a great doctor. He passed away on February 9th 2013.
They donated 131 works to museums and leant over 450 to various exhibitions and museums.
