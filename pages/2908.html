Additional Information

Mr Sandeep Bhammer was named as one of the donors of the new wing at the Herbert F. Johnson Museum of Art of Cornell University, New York. The museum has a space named after his mother, (The Late Mrs Aarti Mahendra) Bhammer Gallery of South Asian Art.
