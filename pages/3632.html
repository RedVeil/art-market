Additional Information

He bought Cezanne's 'Hill of Galet, Pontoise' in 1996 for $11 million, and has since resold it for an estimate of $8 million to $10 million.
He bought Pissarro's 'September Festival, Pontoise' (1872) in 1989 for $1.9 million at Sotheby's, and has since resold it for $2.5 million.
He bought Renoir's 'Women in the Garden' (1873) in 1993 for $6.7 million, the exact price he resold it for.
He held an auction of works from his collection in 2000 at Phillips, the auction house.
