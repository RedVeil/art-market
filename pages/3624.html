Additional Information

According to Forbes, Pinault is the 53rd richest man in the world. His net worth is around $15 billion.
François Pinault's company also controls the auction house Christie’s, a world leader in the art market
On the magazine ArtReview's 2006 list of most powerful people in modern art, Pinault was ranked in first place.
Pinault bought a painting by Rudolf Stingel art Art Basel 2012 for around $3 Million.
Pinault sold Picasso's "Nature morte aux tulips" to Steve Wynn. The purchase price is not known.
François Pinault is set to open his next private museum, Paris Bourse de Commerce, in 2020. It will display the Pinault art collection.
In April of 2019, just hours after the Notre-Dame Cathedral in Paris caught fire, François Pinault and his son François-Henri Pinault pledged €100 million toward rebuilding the Gothic structure.
