Additional Information

He has been collecting art for over 40 years, and bought a work by Toulouse-Lautrec when he was 18.
He has described his own flat as 'the house of a collector' in which he displays his favorite paintings, sculptures and furnishings from the 18th, 19th and 20th centuries.
His collection of art and furnishings is split between the flat which he shares with his partner Pierre Passebon, his own private flat and a home in Portugual.
His home in Portugal houses his collection of Portuguese arts and crafts, including 19th c. ceramics.
