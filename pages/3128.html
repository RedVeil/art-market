Additional Information

He bought his first painting at his former classmate's, Angelos Razis, exhibition, who later became one of Greece's more important painters.
He is considered to be an extroverted collector.
In 2006, the Printmaking Centre Of The Municipality Of Neapolis published a catalogue about the George Kitis Collection: 2006. International Prints. George Kitis' Collection
In 2010 the exhibition at the Macedonian Museum of Contemporary Art was accompanied by a catalogue: Imprint. International prints, multiples and artists' books from the George Kitis' collection.
In 2010,the Municipality of Thessaoniki published a catalogue about works from the Kitis Collection: Greek contemporary painting, the new generation, George Kitis collection, 2010
