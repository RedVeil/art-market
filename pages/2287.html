Additional Information

Catherine and Franck Petitgas are also benefactors of events at the Institut Francais, London.
She is on the the Latin American Acquisitions Committee at the Centre Pompidou, and a member of the International Council of the Tate.
She is to curate the 2013 PINTA Projects, which aim to promote young Latin American artists.
