Additional Information

Dieter Bogner works as an advisor for museums all over the world. Therefore he was involved in the planning of the Bündner Kunstmuseum (Chur), Museum of Christian Georgia (Bodbe), the Polytechnisches Museum (Moskau), Perm Museum XXI (Perm), the Kunsthaus Graz (Graz), Museum of Modern Art (Warsaw), the Museum Folkwang (Essen), the Zentrum Paul Klee (Bern), the Fondation Beyeler (Basel), the Musée Cantonal des Beaux-Arts (Lausanne), the Museumskomplex Ivan Slovtsov (Tjumen), the Kunsthalle (Mannheim), the Contemporary Art Museum (Chengdu), the Museion. Museum für moderne und zeitgenössische Kunst (Bozen), the New Museum of Contemporary Art (New York), Kunstmuseum Liechtenstein (Vaduz) and the Ludwig Museum (Budapest).
The Installations in Schloss Buchberg were especially made for the Collection Dieter und Getraud Bogner.
