Additional Information

Dr. Bulbulia is very private about the size of his collection but his friends said it is â€˜very large.'
He has a cabinet of small curiosities and artworks. He lives and works with his art.
He is a retired general practitioner.
He is involved in many charitable activities.
He serves on the board of WHAT, curates art exhibitions and has served on boards in many areas from the National Art Gallery of Ireland to the Irish Medical Council.
He was one of the principal partners involved in bringing the International Arts in Health Conference to Dublin in 2004.
In 2005, he travelled to Pakistan with friend and art collector Patrick Murphy to buy art. In 2007, they exhibited part of their collections together.
