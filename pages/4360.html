Additional Information

He financed Monica Bonvicini’s installation “She Lies”, which was installed in the harbour in front of the Den Norske Opera and Ballett (Norwegian Opera and Ballet) in May 2010.
He is a collector of art, wine and antique Bergen silver. He has also invested in horse racing and owned the restaurant Bagatelle.
He is said to be one of the top three collectors of contemporary art in Norway.
His restaurant Bagatelle used to serve as a showroom of sorts for Sveaas’ art collection.
Sveaas commissions serval scultpures every year for his scultpure park at his museum. As a prerequisite, the artist must visit the space several times before they make a proposal as Sveaas wants them to be inspired by the place.
Sveaas is the subject of several sculptures at his Kistefos Museum. Marc Quiin replicated his iris for "All of Nature Flows Through Us" and Anne-Karin Furunes created his portrait in her 2003 piece "Christen Sveaas."
