Additional Information

Glen Goei buys exclusively Singaporean art.
In 1994, Glen Goei received the National Youth Award for his contribution to the Arts, as a film and theatre director.
In 2013, Glen Goei was presented in the advertising campaign of Art Stage Singapore as a local art collector/supporter.
