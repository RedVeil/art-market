Additional Information

Each year he throws a party during AIPAD to exhibit his own collection of photographs.
He also supports InMotion benefit photography auctions which provide free legal services to low income women.
He has always been a strong supporter for young contemporary photographers.
His collection mainly consists of images of children and their myriad and relationships with others such as with family, other friends, adults as well as the span of history of medium.
