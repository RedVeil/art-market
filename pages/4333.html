Additional Information

Hans Rasmus Astrup once donated artworks to the "The Art World's Contribution to Fighting the Water Challenge in Kenya" campaign in 2011.
In 2001 Hans Rasmus Astrup paid a record $5.6 million for Jeff Koons' "Michael Jackson and Bubbles" (1988).
It is possible to contact the Astrup Fearnley Collection via it`s curator Hanne Beate Ueland (hb.ueland@fearnleys.no) or it`s director Gunna B. Kvaran (gb.kvaran@fearnleys.no).
