Additional Information

He bought a study for a Festival of Britain mural painting by Keith Vaughan in 2004 for £79,000 and a painting by John Craxton for £38,400. He also bought a watercolour by John Minton for £29,000.
He bought Edward Burra’s 'Ropes and Ladders' in 2005 for £159,000.
He bought Elisabeth Frink's 'Walking Madonna' for a record £377,600 in 2006.
He is particularly active in loaning works to other galleries within the UK, as he wants as many people as possible to enjoy his collection.
His favourite period in art history is the period from the Post-Impressionists through to the end of the Second World War. His favourite artists are Picasso, Van Gogh and Klimt.
