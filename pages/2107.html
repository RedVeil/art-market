Additional Information

She buys only from artists or their galleries, not auction rooms.
She financed Nicole Wermers's 'The Earring' that was placed on the exterior of the Camden Arts Centre in 2006.
She has funded film projects by Daria Martin and Lucile Desamory and hosted private screenings of their work.
She has only ever sold an artwork once, and is reluctant to part with more as she is attached to every piece in the collection.
She has said in the past that her budget is between 5,000 euros and 30,000 euros per work.
She has written a cookbook, 'Valeria Napoleone’s Exquisite Catalogue of Recipes' (Koenig Books: 2012), which includes artworks by artists she is close to.
She lends her collection to various cultural institutions.
She neither buys from art-school degree shows, believing talent has to prove itself first.
