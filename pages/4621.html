Additional Information

Carlos Cojuangco is also known as Charlie Cojuangco.
Collecting art is a passion for him and also a learning process.
He also had the intention to build a museum because he thinks there is too few museums in Manila. He also wants to use the museum to bring art to the masses.
He believes that art is a tool used to train children with critical minds.
He is attracted to any pieces that are colourful, idyllic or any that shows Filippino every-day scenes.
His family has strong inclination in arts. For example, his daughter likes video art and his son likes music.
The first artwork he ever bought was in 1995 and was a Norma Belleza painting.
When collecting, colours and textures are the first things that will draw him to an art piece.
