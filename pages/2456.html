Additional Information

Huang Long and his art asset management agency has been publishing the Gefeng Art Ecology Index since 2009.
Huang Long attended the Second Art Martket Development Summit in 2009 in Shenzhen.
In 2003, Huang Long has sponsored the publication of artist's book "Zhi Xing" ("Quantitative"), for a Chinese experimental ink painting exhibition with the same name.
