Additional Information

She believes that she accidentally became an art collector and she never thought she would become one.
She has also collaborated with Mohammed Daoudi on art projects at the Centrum Foundation in Port Townsend in Washington.
She is an award-wining poet and also is a collector of poems.
She said that she cannot spend that much on purchasing art pieces but if she sees something that she really likes, she would rather spend her money on art and dress 'like a hobo'.
