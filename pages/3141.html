Additional Information

She has a private collection of portraits of herself done by famous artists such as Andy Warhol, Andre Brasilier, Ryuzo Umehara, and others.
She is a founding member of "Hiroshima Museum of Art" in Japan.
She is the wife of Tokushichi Hasegawa, the president of Gallery Nichido and art collector.
She was awarded "Ordre national de la Légion d’honneur" in 2009.
