Additional Information

Sarah and Louis Elson have spoken at a number of events on contemporary collecting, and on the relationship between artists and collectors.
Sarah Elson curated the exhibition 'Fault Line: Art in the Age of Anxiety' for the Bow Arts Trust.
She is a member of the Princeton University Art Museum advisory committee.
She is particularly interested in artworks which people consider challenging. As such, she believes it is important to see that such art contains a person seeking to express themselves. To look at such art becomes an 'exercise in understanding'.
