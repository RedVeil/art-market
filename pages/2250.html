Additional Information

Both Robert Wilson's and Nicky Wilson's parents collect art: His parents collected Irish art, whilst hers collected Scottish art.
Most of the Wilsons' artworks in Jupiter Artland were specifically commissioned for the sites they stand in.
The Wilsons believe private commissions should be seen by as many people as possible, rather than be kept private, where they so often disappear from view.
The Wilsons' love of the land determines their choice of projects, as they look for artworks which respect the landscape and the environment. For them, the art should heighten the landscape's character, just as the landscape should enhance the art.
