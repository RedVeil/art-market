Additional Information

He is a multimillionaire; he made his money with real estate projects.
He was in the jury committee of UPTO35, an international architecture competition for architects under 35, organised by OLIAROS development company (www.upto35.com)
Iasson Tsakonas is a real estate developer and has played host to several contemporary art events.
In 2007 Iasson Tsakonas Firm OLIAROS established the not for profit organization ReMapKM, which organizes bi-annually the contemporary art platform ReMap. The event has since taken place in 2007 and 2009, running in parallel with the Athens Bienniale.
