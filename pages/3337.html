Additional Information

He has been recognized with awards including the Grand Cross of Isabel the Catholic, Grand Cross of the Order of Civil Merit, the prize Juan Lladó and the Gold Medal of the Spanish Institute in New York.
He is the president of the Foundation Príncipe de Asturias.
In 2009 he loaned "The virgin from the Hat" to the Museum of Fine Arts, Bilbao.
