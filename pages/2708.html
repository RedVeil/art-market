Additional Information

Although he first began collecting as an investment, his interests have developed and include paintings and sculptures from across several centuries.
He plans on repeating his Christie's 'Jubilee Line' exhibition for a school audience, as he is keen on using his collection as an educational tool.
Regarding his collection of royal portraits, he is specific as to the criteria he follows when collecting them: the portrait has to have been painted during the monarch's reign, although the monarch doesn’t need to have sat for it.
