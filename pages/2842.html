Additional Information

Konstantinos Papageorgiou has taught philosophy of law at the University of Athens. His publications include Harm and Punishment, Baden-Baden, 1994 (in German), and The Political Possibility of Justice, Athens, 1994 (in Greek), Autonomy and Responsibility, a book of essays on legal, moral and political philosophy.
