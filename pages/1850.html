Additional Information

Grant Hill can be reached at his work phone number (001)407-648-5097
Grant Hill and his wife Tamia established the Tamia and Grant Hill Foundation to support children’s and educational charities.
Among others, his business interests are providing affordable housing and relieving urban food deserts.
Much of the art in Hill’s collection is centered around the cultural heritage of African-American life in the 20th century.
