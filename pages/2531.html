Additional Information

Sacha Craddock is/has been Pubic Art Advisor for the Royal Borough of Kensington and Chelsea.
She gives gallery talks, such as at the Alison Jacques Gallery.
She has written extensively on art and artists, including, in the past, for newspapers such as The Times.
She is/has been curator at the Sadler Wells Arts Centre.
She often appears on panel discussions and on TV to talk about a variety of art related topics, including art's relation to the art market and to the media.
She shares her house in Bloomsbury with a group of artists.
She was Director of Programmes at the Wax Wigram Gallery (2011-2012), and co-curator of Bloomberg Space (2002-2011).
She works as a postgraduate tutor and has been a Visiting Lecturer at the Royal College of Art. She has had a lot of experience accessing degree shows.
When looking at artworks presented at degree shows, rather than look for something completely original, which, in her opinion, is impossible, she appreciates works which demonstrate certain nuances, however slight, and which affirm confidence in these changes.
