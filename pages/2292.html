Additional Information

74% of the value of Barry Lam's collection comes from the works produced by Zhang Daqian.
Barry Lam owns more than 30% of Zhang Daqian's art work.
In 2003, Barry Lam procured all the collection from another famous collecter Cheng Yi and became the biggest art collector in Chinese-speaking region.
In 2010, Barry Lam bought the installation work "Phoenix" (2800x2700 cm) of Xu Bing for 200 million RMB (US$2.93 million), commissioned by Ravenel.
In 2013, Barry Lam is listed as #363 on the Billionaires list (worldwide) and as #5 on Taiwan's Richest on Forbes.
