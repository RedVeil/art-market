Additional Information

David Rockefeller Jr. sold "White Center (Yellow, Pink and Lavender on Rose)" by Mark Rothko at Sotheby`s in 2007. The painting sold for 72.84 million (USD), setting the record of the current most expensive post-war work of art sold at auction.
2018 Christie’s Rockefeller sales total $832.6 million — highest ever for a single collection
