Additional Information

Dr Kenneth Montague is the founder and director of Wedge Curatorial Projects - a project exhibiting photo-based work that explores black identity and the African diaspora over ten years ago. More on this can be found on the website http://www.wedgecuratorialprojects.org/.
Kenneth Montague states in an interview that he is into the following artists at the moment: Megan Morgan, Christina Leslie, Dawit Petros, Stacey Tyrell, Sandra Brewster.
