Additional Information

He has a family fortune of $7.6 billion and a personal fortune of about $4.0 billion.
He descends in the direct male line from merely three of the previous fourteen Princes of Liechtenstein, and of another (again from two of the above mentioned three) in the female line.
He is owner or co-owner of several companies.
He is the reigning Prince of Liechtenstein.
He owns an extensive art collection, which is displayed for the public at the Liechtenstein Museum in Vienna.
He paid €1.5 million ($1.8 million) for Friedrich von Amerling’s Girl in a Straw Hat in 2008.
The Prince of Liechtenstein Foundation is a portfolio of private companies, museums, and art collections owned by the Princely Family of Liechtenstein.
