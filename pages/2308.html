Additional Information

Her vision is to create a country rich in arts and culture.
In 2012, Forbes magazine ranked Sheikha Mayassa bint Hamad al-Thani among the 100 most powerful women of the world. The Economist even describes her "Qatar's culture Queen".
In May 2007 the Royal family of Qatar bought Mark Rothko`s Painting "White Center (Yellow, Pink and Lavender on Rose)" for $72.84 Million. At the same Sotheby`s auction they bought Francis Bacon’s "Study from Innocent X" for $52.7 Million.
The QMA bankrolled Damien Hirst’s Tate retrospective in 2012.
