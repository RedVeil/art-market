Additional Information

In 2008, Wang Zhongjun received Mercedes-AMG National Spirit Achievers Award as one of the most outstanding personalities in the art field.
In 2012, Wang Zhongjun was rated as one of the top 10 personalities who influence the world of art collection in China by Xinhuanet.
Wang Zhongjun occasionally works as an art dealer.
