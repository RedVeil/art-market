Additional Information

Helú holds the biggest collection of works by Auguste Rodin outside France.
Mr Slim purchased his first painting, a 16th-century Flemish work, in the year 1966.
The Hispanic Society of America awarded Carlos Slim Foundation the “Sorolla Medal” by its arts and culture support October 6th, 2011, New York, NY.
