Additional Information

He and his wife, Mary Pappajohn, donated more than 24 artworks to Des Moines Art Centre in Des Moines, Iowa. These artworks are now worth approximately $24 million. The sculptures donated are kept in the Papajohn Sculpture Park ad is open for public viewing. The sculptures are from artists such as Edward Hopper, Roy Lichtenstein, Henri Matisse and Georgia O’Keefe.
