Additional Information

Walter Vanhaerents' collection is heavily influenced by Andy Warhol and pop art.
Walter Vanhaerents does not buy second-hard art.
The Vanhaerents Art Collection is open to the public by appointment just four times annually, plus ten weeks for tours and schools. There are no labels; all visits are guided.
Somewhat notoriously, Walter Vanhaerents will not reveal how many artworks are in the collection.
