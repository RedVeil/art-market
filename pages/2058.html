Additional Information

He donated works of art for the '24 Hour Auction' to raise money for the Camden Arts Centre.
He has also supported the artist Tereza Buskova with funding and opportunities.
He is also a patron of the Camden Arts Centre where he supports the Outset Residency Programme.
Rob Diament is a patron of public art spaces in London including the South London Gallery, Chisenhale Gallery, The Showroom, Studio Voltaire, and also Institute of Contemporary Arts, performing live at their 2010 fundraising gala.
