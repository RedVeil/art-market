Additional Information

He has his own vineyard in Bordeaux, Chateau de Fieuzal, which he bought in 2001.
He is a brother of Ruairi Quinn, and first cousin of Senator Feargal Quinn.
He is a member of the Irish Advisory Board of the UCD Michael Smurfit Graduate School of Business.
He is main benefactor of the UCD Quinn School of Business.
He is part-owner of the luxury Merrion Hotel in the centre of Dublin, where he houses a number of paintings from his own collection.
His mother liked to have paintings at home and his brother Ruairi was a talented artist but gave it up in his Twenties.
Lochlann Quinn bought a painting by Louis le Brocquy in London for £1.7m, and with his wife, Brenda, presented it to the National Gallery of Ireland.
