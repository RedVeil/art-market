Additional Information

In June 2012 Siegfried Loch sold 45 artworks of his collection at Christie's including artwork from Gerhard Richter, «Abstraktes Bild», 1995 (1,45 Million Euros), Yves Klein, «Anthropométrie» (1,6 Million Euros), Andreas Gursky, «Ayamonte», 1955 (91250 Pounds), Jörg Immendorff, «Sammler» (25000 Pounds).
