Additional Information

Franks and Suss hold very firm principles when selecting works of art for their collection, looking for “genuine artistic skill and technical brilliance, an innovative and original concept and a challenging or aesthetically pleasing look.” They pride themselves on collecting works by artists "when they were yet to be discovered or were still on the cusp of international recognition." "We have been contemplating for a while to build a space for the Franks – Suss collection."
