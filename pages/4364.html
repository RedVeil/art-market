Additional Information

La Fondation de la Famille Claudine et Stephen Bronfman has put aside a fund of 550 000$ in order to support, yearly, 2 artists, one from Concordia, the other from l'UQAM, in launching their careers.
The SéminArts, which their foundation runs in collaboration with le Musée d'art contemporain de Montréal, seeks to discover, attract and educate new art collectors with visits to galleries, as well as to corporate and private collections.
