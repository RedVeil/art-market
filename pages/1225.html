Additional Information

Ganek and his wife were founding members of the leadership committee for the exhibition "Maurizio Cattelan: All" at Guggenheim in 2011.
Ganek commissioned painter Ed Ruscha to create a work that incorporated the word "Level"; it hangs in the firm's Greenwich headquarters.
Ganek purchased his first painting at the age of 17.
