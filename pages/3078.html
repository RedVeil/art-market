Additional Information

A video showing al Tuki`s interests concerning artists can be seen here: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qBtCgzKbqeE
He has co-edited 'Edge of Arabia' (Booth-Clibborn, 2012).
He has talked at Art Basel about Middle Eastern Art in relation to Patronage and Politics (2011).
He wishes to use art to encourage people to understand his country and religion, which he believes are so often misrepresented.
Through Edge of Arabia, he played a role in the creation of The Crossway Foundation, the first UK-Middle Eastern Education and Arts Centre, set up in partnership with the Prince’s Regeneration Trust and Offscreen.
Through his role as Creative Director at Edge of Arabia, he was involved in setting up 'The Future of a Promise', the first pan-Arab exhibition of contemporary art at the 54th Venice Biennale (2011).
