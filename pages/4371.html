Additional Information

He has been the patron of the British Shakespeare Company.
He inherited his collection from his father Thomas Fredrik Olsen.
He is businessman, billionaire and member of the Olsen shipping family, who own Fred. Olsen & Co.
He owns 38 artworks by Edvard Munch, 20 of them are oil paintings.
He sold Munch's The Scream to fund a Munch museum housing the family collection at Petter Olsen’s country house in Norway.
His father, Thomas Fredrik Olsen, was a friend and patron of Edvard Munch.
Petter Olsen formerly owned one of the four versions of Edvard Munch's The Scream (1895), before he sold it at an auction for record price of US$119.9 million. He sold the painting to raise funds to build a museum in Hvitsten, Norway.
