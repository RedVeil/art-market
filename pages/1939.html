Additional Information

According to Forbes, Walton has a fortune of $33.5 billion.
Alice Walton purchased Edward Hopper`s painting Blackwell’s Island for $19,163,750 at Christie’s New York in 2013.
In 2005, Walton purchased Asher Brown Durand's celebrated painting, Kindred Spirits, in an auction for a purported US$35 million dollars.
In 2013 Alice Walton acquired the sculpture "Hanging Heart" by Jeff Koons.
In November 2013, Walton purchased Warhol`s "Coca Cola (3)" at Christie`s for $57,2 million.
She also bought a Jasper Johns “Alphabets” painting from 1960-62 for $11 Million.
The Crystal Bridges Collection focusses on American art.
