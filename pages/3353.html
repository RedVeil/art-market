Additional Information

His family has been collecting contemporary artworks, artifacts, folk art objects and ancient art for over one hundred years.
Mr. Pieridis has a big collection of Greek contemporary art, especially a collection of modern Greek engravings.
Mr. Pieridis set up the Pierides Foundation in 1974 in memory of his ancestors.
