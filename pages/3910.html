Additional Information

Mr Murray Quinn sponsored an art event in 2006 where visual art students of the Grand Prairie Campus had the opportunity to submit their original drawings, paintings or graphics which will be hung in the Prairie Gallery. The works were then scanned and made into limited edition buttons.
Mr Murray Quinn was a panel member at a discussion at Art Toronto 2011 titled "Collect Call: Collectors Speak".
