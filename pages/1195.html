Additional Information

In 2003, the late medieval Fürstlich Fürstenberg Collection of Paintings was acquired and incorporated into the Würth Collection.
In 2010, Würth discovered that he owned two forgeries resembling works by Heinrich Campendonk and Max Ernst. Both works were painted by the same forger, Wolfgang Beltracchi, who was later sentenced to six years in jail.
