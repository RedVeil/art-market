Additional Information

He started his collection with a painting by Greek artist Yannis Tsarouchis in 1987.
He was a sponsor of the more important exhibitions at the Athens National Gallery.
In 2000 he opened the Portalakis Collection Exhibition Space to the public, and has been showing one to two exhibitions a year with works exclusively from his collection.
Portalakis is a professional broker.
The Portalakis Collection began to be formed in 1987 with Greek artists, went on with expatriated Greek artists, and from 1991 on with European and American artists.
Zacharias Portalakis is the world's largest collector of works by Greek artist Theodoros Stamos (200 paintings and 120 drawings).
