Additional Information

He is a member of TUSIAD (Turkish Industry and Business Association), FB 1907 and TGSD (Turkish Clothing Manufacturers Association).
He is one of the leading art collectors in Turkey.
In 1999 he scooped the Ready to Wear Award at the Istanbul Fashion Awards for Turkish Pioneers.
In 2004 he was recognized for his role in art collection and received the Istanbul Contribution to Art Award.
Tavilo?lu is also a recipient of the coveted 2007 Retail Sun Lifetime Achievement Award.
