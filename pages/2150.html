Additional Information

Cathy Wills's interest in the arts began in the 1970s, when working with Henry Lydiate at ArtLaw. She went on to found the Lewis Johnson Gallery, of which she was Director (1978-1984). She was also on the Board of Cornerhouse, Manchester (1984-2005), and a Council Member of the North West Arts Board.
She co-curated 'Intersections: Science in Contemporary Art' at the Weizmann Institute of Science (2012) with Carolyne Drake.
She supports the Contemporary Art Society's Sculpture Fund, for she believes in the importance of supporting regional museums acquire the best of contemporary art. She has said that, through this fund, she wishes to encourage other collectors and philanthropists to look outside of London.
