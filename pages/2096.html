Additional Information

Lauren Prakke was on the Advisory Committees for the Whitechapel Gallery's one-off events 'Art Plus Music Party' (22 April 2010); 'TOD'S Art Plus Drama Party' (24 March 2011); 'Swarovski Art Plus Opera' (15 March 2012).
One of Prakke Contemporary projects was "Play", a "pop-up" gallery, conceived with Nick Hackworth, director of Paradise Row, in a disused mansion at 50 Upper Brooke Street.
Prakke Contemporary is currently in talks with Canvas TV about launching a TV show in which artists are interviewed in their studios.
She was a Committee member for 'Pandamonium 2012', an evening of performances, with an open air catwalk show and an auction by Phillips de Pury to raise funds and highlight the work of Pandamonium, a group which creates wearable sculpture.
She was a panelist on Three Sixty Bespoke's second installment of the panel series Fizzy Water, entitled 'Form - Function: a discussion of emerging trends in contemporary European sculpture.'
She was on the Committee for 'INTERCOURSE', a day-long event at the ICA (26 November 2011).
She was on the Honorary Committee for the 'Artists for Women for Women International' auction, held during the Frieze Art Fair as part of Christie’s Post War and Contemporary Day Sale to raise money for Women for Women International.
