Additional Information

The David Roberts collection is not focused on a specific medium, generation or geographical area.
The David Roberts Foundation is a well-known registered charity in England and Wales in London.
The Kadist Art Foundation (Paris), Nomas Foundation (Rome) and David Roberts Art Foundation (London) offer their collections as a research tool, to develop an exchange on notions of collection/collecting through an interdisciplinary curatorial fellowship.
