Additional Information

He curated two exhibitions in 2010-11, both at la Maison Hamel-Bruneau, Québec, which led to his being nominated for the title of 'Young Curator of the Year' by AGAC: the first entitled 'Concerto en bleu majeur' on the connection between the visual arts and music; the second, 'Photographes rebelles à l'époque de la Grande Noirceur (1937-1961)'.
