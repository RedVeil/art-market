Additional Information

In 2008 they established the "Jane and Marc Nathanson Distinguished Artist in Residence Program" at the Aspen Art Museum.
Jane and Marc Nathanson donated $10 Million for contemporary art programs and acquisitions to LACMA. Therefore the "Jane and Marc Nathanson Gallery of LACMA's Broad Contemporary Art Museum was named after them.
