Additional Information

Klüser was highly influential in Andy Warhol's career. He encouraged Warhol to experiment through different media to develop the portrait image of Lenin.
in 2016, Bernd Klüser received the Faega Lifetime Award, which distinguishes exemplary work of European galleries.
