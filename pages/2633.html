Additional Information

He has a full-time art advisor, Dimitris Gravanis, who is actually the one bidding for the art.
His collection lacks focus and structure apart from a penchant for Austro-German art.
Economou owns the world's largest collection of Otto Dix prints, and has about 500 works currently on loan to the State Museum in Munich.
George Economou bought a wooden sculpture from 1924 by Hermann Scherer for over € 1 million at Art Basel in 2012.
He buys with his eyes first.
He is a self made shipping magnate; his net worth is $ 1.7 billion. He has been ranked among the world’s 1,000 wealthiest individuals.
He is interested in almost every area of art history.
He is known for buying from smaller exhibition spaces and auction houses in Germany and Austria, also known as an uncommon collector.
In 2010 he auctioned Lucas Cranach the Younger for € 650.000.
