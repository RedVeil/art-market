Additional Information

As he mostly collects works on paper, he is especially aware of having to be careful about the amount of natural light which he exposes his collection to.
He displays about 40 works from his collection in various galleries; whilst the remaining ones are hung in his home or placed in storage nearby.
He is interested in the local art scene and always purchases artworks in person.
He was director of l'Association des galeries d'art contemporain (AGAC) and head of the Musée des Beaux-Arts de Montréal. He also founded La Société des Arts sur papier, which existed between 2004 and 2006.
His first major purchase was in 2004 for the sum of 500$. He has since built up a collection of over 68 works of art, valued for 100 000$.
