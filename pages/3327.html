Additional Information

In 2007 Schnitzer donated $5,000 to the Portland Art Center.
With her husband Harold Schnitzer who died in 2011 she created a collection of pre-Han and Han ceramics that led to the creation of the "Arlene and Harold Schnitzer Collection of Early Chinese Art".
