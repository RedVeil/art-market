Additional Information

Mr. and Mrs. Polsky donated between $1,000 to $4,999 to the Frick Collection in 2010-2011.
She and her husband Leon B. Polsky supported the exhibition "Tomás Saraceno on the Roof: Cloud City" at the Metropolitan Museum of Art in 2012.
The exhibition "Cambodian Rattan: The Sculptures of Sopheap Pich" at Metropolitan Museum of Art in 2013 was made possible by Cynthia Hazen Polsky and Leon B. Polsky.
