Additional Information

Norman Dubrow has been credited with introducing the works of Jean-Michel Basquiat to collectors Hubert and Dolores Ormandy Neumann.
Norman Dubrow has a reputation for being ahead of curve in art collecting. He was one of the very first collectors, if not the first, to buy works by Christian Schumann, Inka Essenhigh, Michael Bevilacqua, Wolfgang Tillmans, Katy Grannan, Ingrid Calam√© and Arturo Elizondo.
Norman Dubrow is also known for being very quick to donate items from his collection to museums.
