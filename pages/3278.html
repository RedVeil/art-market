Additional Information

As an art critic and curator, Eugene Tan was listed among the "50 movers and shakers of Singapore's art scene" in 2011.
Eugene Tan was the co-curator of the inaugural Singapore Biennale in 2006 and curator for the Singapore Pavilion at the 2005 Venice Biennale. He curated and represented the Singapore conceptual artist Lim Tzay Chuen at the 2005 Venice Biennale.
Eugene Tan's last purchase was a drawing by German artist Carsten Nicolai at Art Stage Singapore.
