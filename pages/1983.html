Additional Information

He published an essay about Korean art market and his collecting story in 2011.
He was born in Busan. He donated his art collections to Busan and Gyeongnam provincial museum for art development and citizens.
In particular, he has been collecting many modern Japanese artists. He bought 100 modern Japanese art works for donating in 2009.
Shin donated many artworks since 1998. He donated 350 artworks to Busan City Museum , to Gyeongnam Art Museum 220 pieces of art works, to Miryang City Museum 100 pieces of art works, to Busan Museum of Art more than 50 pieces of art works.
