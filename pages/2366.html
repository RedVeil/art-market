Additional Information

In october 2013 Antoine de Galbert bought a drawing by Unica Zürm at the FIAC in Paris.
The collection of Antoine de Galbert and the foundation La Maison rouge are two different entities, even if they are closely linked.
The collection of Antoine de Galbert is focused on outsider art.
