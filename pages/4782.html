Additional Information

Apart from art collecting, she and her husband, Alan Stein, run their own program called the Stein Program where it aids and offers scholarships to young people and students in order to help them pursue their careers and increase their future family security.
She and her husband have now focused on support emerging artists whose artworks are forms of media such as video, fluorescent, and glass.
She and her husband, Alan Stein collect together of such Bay Area artists.
