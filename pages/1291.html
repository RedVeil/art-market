Additional Information

Thomas Olbricht has been collecting Gerhard Richter editions for over 20 years. He managed to acquire all of his editions except one.
Wolfgang Schoppmann is the head curator of Olbricht Collection and a long time friend.
In 2011, Thomas Olbricht founded his private museum, me Collectors Room Berlin. However, it closed permanently in 2020.
