Additional Information

For a long time, being an art conservator, she has been promoting the idea of preserving visual and cultural heritage.
It is possible to contact Suzanne deal Booth via http://www.sdbooth.com/blog/contact/.
One of the first paintings that she and her husband were collecting was Venus Blue by Yves Klein and produced posthumously by Jean-Paul Ledeur.
She grew up going to museums but none of her family members were collecting art back then.
She has recently sponsored the Louise Bourgeois Exhibit at the Museum of Contemporary Art in Los Angeles as well as the visiting artist program at the Pont Aven School of Art in Brittany
With the same passion for art, she and husband both created the Booth Heritage Foundation and then later on founded the The Friends of Heritage Preservation (FOHP) which serves to respond to the needs of preservation within the US and as well as in other countries.
