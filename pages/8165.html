Additional Information

Michael Xufu Huang was a co-founder of MWOODs art museum in Beijing but has since moved on to open up his own private museum with co-founder Theresa Tse.
Huang served on the New Museum's International Leadership Council for teo years before being named the youngest member of the institution’s board of directors.
Huang’s first acquisition was a lithograph by Color Field artist Helen Frankenthaler, a gift from his parents in 2010 when he was 16 years old.
Huang was named as one of Forbes’ 30 under 30 in 2017.
Huang curated Nicolas Party’s first solo exhibition in a museum in China.
One of the most talked-about acquisitions of Huang's is Amalia Ulman's 'Excellences & Perfections' (2014), a kind of digital performance art that was uploaded to various social media sites over the span of five months by the artist.
