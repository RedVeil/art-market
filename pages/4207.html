Additional Information

The 1976 silkscreen portrait of Empress Farah Pahlavi was ripped to shreds by an angry crowd in the early days of the Iranian revolution—luckily, one of the only works in the collection to be maimed.
The collection built by Empress Farah Pahlavi during her reign, which still remain in Iran, has an estimated worth of US$3 billion (S$4.08 billion).
