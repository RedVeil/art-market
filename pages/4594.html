Additional Information

Danald L Bryant sold Francis Bacon`s "Study for a Portrait" for $4.4 million at Christie`s in February 2014. He had bought it for $4.2 million in 2012.
Donald L. Bryant Jr. attended the Museum of Modern Art's 36th Annual Party (honoring Steve Martin) in 2004.
Donald L. Bryant Jr. owned Andy Warhol 's "Marlon" until he sold it at Christie’s in 2012.
Donald L. Bryant, Jr. purchased the work "Milanese Piazza" by Gerhard Richter for $37.1 million at Sotheby's in 2013.
In 2013, Henry Kravis, co-chairman of KKR & Co. (KKR), sued Donald L. Bryant Jr. claiming he reneged on an agreement to donate paintings by Jasper Johns to the Museum of Modern Art in New York. Donald L. Later Bryant Jr. said MoMA would get these artworks as promised, just with 1 month's delay time.
