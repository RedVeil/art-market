Additional Information

Her net worth alone is €550 million, with her husband $2.4 billion.
She goes officially by the name of Lady Chryss O'Reilly.
She has been Chairperson of the O'Reilly Foundation since its establishment (www.oreillyfoundation.ie). The foundation support research and education.
She holds both USA and Greek citizenship.
She is a Greek shipping heiress, horse owner and breeder.
She is heavily involved with Ireland Funds.
She is the wife of media and industrial magnate Tony O'Reilly.
She studied history of art at the Sorbonne.
Tony O'Reilly and Chryss Goulandris married in 1991.
