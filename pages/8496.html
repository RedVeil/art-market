Additional Information

Nachson Mimran is an investor, entrepreneur and creative activist who has wholeheartedly taken on the challenge of tackling climate change and humanitarian crises.
Mimran often displays works from his collection at his family's hotel, The Alpina Gstaad, in the Swiss Alps. This includes the #TOGETHERBAND TAPESTRY, an artwork that inspires the viewer to reflect upon the UN’s 17 Sustainable Development Goals.
