Additional Information

Jean Pigozzi has an African Art collection together with André Magnin, called C.A.A.C. In addition to that, he owns the Japigozzi collection of Japanese art.
In 2016, Jean Pigozzi published a book titled Pool Party. It documents the various guests who have visited his Côte d’Azur home over the years.
