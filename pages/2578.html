Additional Information

Denyse and Philippe Durand-Ruel have a son who is the director of the contemporary art department at Christie’s.
Denyse Durand-Ruel belongs to the same family as Paul Durand-Ruel (1831-1922), a famous French art dealer who is associated with the Impressionists.
Paul-Louis Durand-Ruel and Flavie Durand-Ruel are also taking care of the Durand-Ruel archives.
