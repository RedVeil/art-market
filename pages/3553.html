Additional Information

Mr. Magnier has also been a Senator in the upper house of the Irish Parliament, Seanad Éireann.
His net worth is €2.56 billion (2020).
In 2002, he spent $4.5m on a few paintings by Alfred Munnings showing his favourite animal (horse).
Mr. Magnier is considered to be the most influential man in horseracing and breeding worldwide. He is also known as The Boss.
In 2018, John Magnier sold the 1917 Amedeo Modigliani nude ‘Nu couche’ (Reclining Nude) for $157.2 million, making it the most expensive artwork ever sold by Sotheby’s.
