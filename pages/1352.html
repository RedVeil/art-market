Additional Information

Harry Half, her son, is the owner of Harry Halff Fine Art -- specializing in nineteenth century American and European Art.
Husband (deceased) served on the American Art Advisory Committee of the Dallas Museum of Art, the Board of Directors of the Amon Carter Museum, Board of Directors of the Archives of American Art, Board of Governors of the Smithsonian American Art Museum, and Board of Directors of the San Antonio Museum of Art.
In 2011 she made a donation through the directors of donation circle to the San Antonio Museum of Art.
In 2013 Halff supported the exhibition "REAL/SURREAL SELECTIONS FROM THE WHITNEY MUSEUM OF AMERICAN ART" at the McNay Art Museum.
