Additional Information

Brant co-produced Andy Warhol: A Documentary (2006) a Peabody- and Emmy-award winning PBS documentary. H was executive producer of Basquiat (1996) and Pollock (2000); both award winning films.
Brant is the publisher of "Interview Magazine" and "Art" in America.
Has over 2000 artworks by Andy Warhol.
Mr. Brant was honored by The Whitney Museum at the 21st Annual American Art Awards.
On the 12th of November the sculpture "Balloon Dog" by Jeff Koons from Peter Brant`s collection was sold at Christie`s for $58,405,000.
Brant also has commissioned major works by artists like Urs Fischer and Maurizio Cattelan.
