Additional Information

He made two art purchases: a female portrait by Emil Nolde ("Nadja"), which he auctioned in 2007 for € 2.6 million at Ketterer Kunst in Munich and August Macke's "Still Life with apple peel and Japanese subjects", acquired in 2008 for the equivalent of € 1.8 million at Sotheby's in London.
Jürgen Hall is a patron of the "Kunstmuseum Bonn" and has purchased several artworks for the Museum.
