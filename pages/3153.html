Additional Information

Although he does sell on some paintings, he has never sold an object from his collection.
He does not see himself as a collector, believing that the term focusses too much on the object and not enough on the act of collecting, the when and how of it, which, for him, is as important.
He has been a Fellow of the Royal Society since 2006.
