Additional Information

Collecting art is the "psychic link" between her and her husband and that it pervades their plans.
She bought her first art piece in her collection in 2001 when she was out on a date in Malate with her husband, Luis Campos.
She grew up being surrounded by her mother's floral art pieces.
She now has been collecting with her husband for more than 10 years.
She received a gift from the president Cory Aquino a painting as their wedding gift.
When buying an art piece, she and her husband look at whether they like the piece or not and then the price.
