Additional Information

Marie Donnelly is former chairwoman of the Irish Museum of Modern Art, she had to step down in 2001.
Mrs. Donnelly is considered a socialite.
The Donnellys are among only a tiny handful of Irish collectors to collect international modern and contemporary art.
