Additional Information

Artur Jorge Braga Melo Teixeira is commonly known as simply Artur Jorge.
In 2010 around 100 artworks of his collection were auctioned off in Paris. Among them, there were pieces by Dalí, Chagall, Picasso, Basquiat, Kandisky, Warhol, Magritte, Man Ray, Klein, Pollock. He said he auctioned these artworks so he could have space to buy more and invest in Portuguese art.
