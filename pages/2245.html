Additional Information

Fré Ilgen has also gained experience as a curator. He curated various exhibitions since 1983.
Ilgen collects together with his wife Jacqueline.
Next to being an art collector Fré Ilgen is an artists himself.
The Ilgens host the private art event "Checkpoint Ilgen" two times a year.
