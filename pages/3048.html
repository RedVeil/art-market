Additional Information

He has a very eclectic collection spanning from 1000BC to the present day. It includes Modernism, inter-war European photography, 20th-century American modernism, antiquities and contemporary Latin American and international art.
He keenly attends art fairs, particularly Maastricht and Basel where he often buys works as there the very best are for sale in his opinion, and also attends but does not tend to buy from Frieze, TEFAF and Miami.
