Additional Information

The London’s School of Oriental and African Studies has awarded Professor Nirmalya Kumar an honorary fellowship in recognition of his patronage and promotion of South Asian Art in 2012.
Nirmalya Kumar's Jamini Roy collection is considered the largest outside of India. It has been painstakingly pieced from different stages of the artist’s career, making a conscious effort to represent all his styles.
Nirmalya Kumar's Jamini Roy collection (70+ works) is housed in London’s Baker Street.
Nirmalya Kumar became an art collector by accident after seeing an advertisement in an inflight magazine in 1997.
