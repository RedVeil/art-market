Additional Information

Franz Armin Morat takes care of the artistic work of the Italian painter and printmaker Giorgio Morandi.
In 1995 Morat auctioned off 13 paintings, 22 watercolors, 35 drawings and etchings by Giorgio Morandi for a total of 3,2 million pounds.
The Morat-Institut collection also includes masks and sculptures from Burkina Faso.
