Additional Information

He hopes to open a gallery in his native Tehran soon. In the meantime, 80-90% of the work he owns is in storage.
He owns several hundred artworks.
Shariat believes the key to finding a 'winning' artist is through proper research: to visit as many art openings, fairs and museum exhibitions as one can, and to talk to dealers, artists and collectors about the art.
When Amir Shariat spots a promising new artists, he calls his collector friends. They are a group of 5 people, including Anita Zabludowicz and Fatima and Eskander Malek, who regularly meet to discuss artists.
