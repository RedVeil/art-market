Additional Information

A visit to Pakistan in 2005 made a strong impression on him, leading him to state his interest in owning artworks by Pakistani artists. He was particulalry taken at the time by works by the artists Gul Jee and Saeed Anwar.
He catalogued, and published, the works of the artist Patrick Tuohy.
He has also been involved with The Northern Ireland Arts Council Visual Arts Committee, the Department of Foreign Affairs Cultural Relations Committee and the Organisation of the Visual Arts Exhibitions in Brussels in both 1990 and 1996, to mark Ireland's Presidency of the European Union.
He has stated in an interview that, were he to have the funds to, he would chose to purchase works by Pierre Bonnard, Franz Kline and Mark Rothko.
Since 2000, he has been Art Advisor to both the Office of Public Works, where he works in ensuring the best of contemporary Irish art is displayed in state buildings, and the President of Ireland.
