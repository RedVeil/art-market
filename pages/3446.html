Additional Information

He also had an impressive collection of marble busts at Fota. When University College Cork was forced to put Fota House on the market in 1987, Richard Wood removed his collection and placed it between University College Limerick (on loan) and his own home, not far from Fota. In 2007, the busts were offered for sale through Christie’s London.
He is close to the Contemporary Irish Art Society (CIAS), and invites members to his house to show his collection.
He leased Fota House from University College Cork in 1974 and began to fill the rooms with paintings, sculptures, furniture and other objects.
He owns a significant collection of 18th, 19th and 20th century landscape paintings.
Mr. Richard Wood is a noted businessman and collector.
