Additional Information

Besides collecting fine art, Catherine Woodard also write poetry and play basketball. She has published several books on poetry and held the fellowships at Virginia Center for the Creative Arts in 2011. She worked previously as a reporter/editor for several media and now serves on the board of Poetry Society of America (since 2011).
Nelson Blitz Jr. and Catherine Woodard were listed as #9 biggest collectors in the United States and #19 worldwide in 2012, released at ARTnews.
