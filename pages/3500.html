Additional Information

She especially enjoys 'thinking' artists who are not yet established, and bringing them to other people’s attention in her own gallery space in London.
She has set up her own gallery in what used to be the ballroom in her l8th-century London house, and holds four to five appointment-only exhibitions a year. One of these exhibitions, curated by the art advisor Count Manfredi della Gherardesca of MDG Fine Arts, showed works by the young artist Adam Ball.
