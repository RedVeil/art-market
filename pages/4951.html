Additional Information

Len Blavatnik is a member of the Board of Governors of The New York Academy of Sciences
Leonard Blavatnik`s nickname is Len.
In 2017, Tate Modern’s Switch House extension was renamed the Blavatnik Building after he donated over £50 million.
In 2016, The V&A’s new Exhibition Road entrance was named after Blavatnik after his foundation donated funds, rumoured to be around £5 million.
In 2017, Blavatnik was knighted by the queen for his philanthropy.
