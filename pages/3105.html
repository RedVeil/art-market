Additional Information

As part of her role at Arts Co., she helped produce the London Design Festival (2011).
She believes that it is important to buy artworks which one likes, especially works which one cannot imagine oneself living without. She advises to buy according to one's budget whilst exploring before buying, as well as to ask questions, educate one's eye and, if possible, to get to know the artist.
She has given a number of talks on collecting, including one on the links between the art and luxury goods markets for ArtInsight (2013).
She has said that she buys artworks infrequently but quickly, and gets a rush out of it.
The most she has spent in one go on artworks is £5,000.
