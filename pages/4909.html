Additional Information

in 2015, Liu Yiqian stunned the art world with his $170 million (£113 million) purchase of Amedeo Modigliani's Reclining Nude.
Liu also collects antique porcelain and is rumoured to be the buyer of the iny Ru-ware brush washer from the Northern Song dynasty that broke the auction record for the world’s most expensive ceramic in 2017.
One of Liu's most famous acquisitions was the tiny Ming Dynasty “Chicken Cup” for which he paid US$36 million in 2014 at Sotheby’s before drinking tea from it – causing a social media meltdown.
