Additional Information

He was the first to reveal Man Ray's real name (Emmanuel Radnitzky), in his book "Man Ray's works and life" (1977)
He was the owner of Gallery Schwarz- Art Gallery and a publishing house.
He won 2006 Italy's Premio Frascati prize for his collected works of poetry
On 6 March 1998 he was awarded the "Diploma of First Class with gold medal for outstanding merits in the fields of culture and the arts".
Over the years, he donated more than 700 artworks of Dada and Surrealist art to the Israel Museum in Jerusalem.
