Additional Information

He owns his own collection of artworks which he has on display in his San Francisco loft. He seeks to collect 20th and 21st-century furniture and sculpture, letting their contrasts play off against each other, highlighting, as such, their different artistries.
Steven Volpe and his business partner Roth Martin share a Paris pied-à-terre, which they use on their regular business trips. It is filled with artworks they have assembled together to decorate it. They have done the same with a country retreat in Northwest Marin County, north of San Francisco’s Golden Gate Bridge, which they co-own along with Martin's wife and children.
They both share a taste for mid-century French and Italian designs, and like to purchase 'noniconic' pieces that provoke a reaction.
