Additional Information

Haro Cumbusyan and Bilge Ogut-Cumbusyan are particularly interested in works which involve new media and emerging technology; an interest which comes from both their professional backgrounds in technology.
Haro Cumbusyan has been on panels and given talks on art collecting, emerging art-markets and supporting contemporary artists, such as at Art Dubai and Art Taipei 2012.
The Bilge & Haro Cumbusyan Collection / Collectorspace received the 'Award for Initiative in Collecting' from the Friends of ARCO Association in 2013.
