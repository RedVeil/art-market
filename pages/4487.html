Additional Information

Nathalie de Gunzburg received the medal of Chevalier of the Order of Arts and Letters in 2013 from Antonin Baudry, Cultural Counselor of the French Embassy at the French Embassy Cultural Services in New York for her achievement in art collecting and serving as the Chair of DIA Art Foundation.
