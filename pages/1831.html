Additional Information

As a private museum owner, Dai Zhikang attended the 2013 Private Museum Summit in London.
Himalaya Art Museum is a private contemporary art museum owned by Dai Zhikang's Zendai Group, with more than 7,000 sqm exhibition space in Shanghai. The building was designed by the famous architect Arata Isozaki.
In a charity auction in 2010, Dai Zhikang bought Zeng Fanzhi's "Tai Ping You Xiang"(2008) with CNY 10 million (USD 1.6 million).
