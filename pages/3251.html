Additional Information

He is the mayor of the cities Varis - Voula - Vouliagmeni in Greece.
Spyros Panos, along with his brother Chrysanthos, has contributed in shaping the trends and style of catering and hospitality in Greece for the last 20 years with their company Panos Group. They exhibit their art collection in the places they own.
