Additional Information

Besides collecting art, he also collects sport jerseys, sport figurines, and sporting icons.
He dedicated a whole mezzanine floor of 320 sq foot to store his art collection.
He got awarded the Most Outstanding Filipino in Singapore (MOFS) in 2005.
He often goes with his wife to galleries in Singapore to keep updated with the latest pieces.
He prefers to support Filippino artworks.
His bigger pieces in the collection are stored in Helutrans which is an art storage space near Tanjong Pagar.
His first floor of his home contains BenCab's artworks.
In 1991, he covered a segment at the Luz Gallery to cover a painter's exhibit.
In 2006, he was awarded with TOYM (Ten Outstanding Young Men).
