Additional Information

Nadia Samdani and her husband, Rajeeb, are founding members of the Tate South Asian Arts Council, the Tate International Council and Harvard University's South Asian Arts Council.
In 2015, Nadia Samdani and her husband sponsored the Bangladeshi visual artist Naeem Mohaiemen and the Raqs Media Collective in the 56th International Biennale di Venezia.
In 2014, Nadia Samdani and her husband were nominated in the Young Collector of the Year category for the Forbes India Art Award.
