Additional Information

He is a big fan of the Korean artist Chang Ucchin. He became Chang's friend by writing to him in 1973. After Chang's death, Kim published his memories of the friendship with Chang.
Kim is a professor of environmentology. Sometimes he does special lectures about art collecting too.
