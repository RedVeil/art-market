Additional Information

Charles Asprey has curated several shows, including 'As If By Magic' (2006) with Kay Pallister at The Peace Centre in Bethlehem; the exhibition of new work by Alessandro Raho at Thomas Williams Fine Art, Old Bond Street (2008); and 'The Ground Around: idylls, earthworks and thunderbolts' at Vilma Gold Gallery, London (2010).
He established the artist residency programme 'Randolph Cliff' in Edinburgh with Dr Clmentine Deliss.
He was the co-owner and co-director of aspreyjacques, a London art gallery, which showed works by emerging German artists.
