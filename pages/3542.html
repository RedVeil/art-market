Additional Information

In 1991, she set up a not for profit organization called First Step. The aim of First-Step is to provide microfinance loans to start up or expanding small and medium enterprises for job creation or protection.
She is Michael Smurfit's ex-wife.
She is very well known for her philanthropic work in Ireland. A library at the National College of Ireland is named after her.
