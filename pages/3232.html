Additional Information

Damien Hirst sold Jeff Koons “Cracked Egg (Magenta)" at Christie`s in February 2014 for $23.3 million.
He believes the essence of the act of creation is the conception, rather than the execution of a work.
He enjoys exhibiting his private collection, believing it is like a mapping process of both his life as an artist and as a collector.
He has donated four of his own works to the Tate, London, with the intention of giving more in the future, in order to bring the gift to a total of 30 donated works.
He has had several plans to create an exhibition space for his collection, in imitation of Charles Saatchi: one at Toddington Manor, near Cheltenham; and in a gallery in Vauxhall, London.
He sees his collecting not as something planned or managed, but as the result of his being human, for he believes humans naturally go through life amassing and collecting objects.
