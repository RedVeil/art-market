Additional Information

Before his current gallery, his exhibition space was a classroom and even then he had five major and five minor shows although it was a small exhibition space.
He did a "Walking Tour" with workshops, demonstrations, and lectures at the 5-day event of National Commission of Culture and the Arts (NCCA) for ManilART 09
He has donated what is left from his collection to the Fatima University Gallery in order for it to be used as an education tool for the future generations.
He prefers to collect paintings from Filipino artists.
When he first started collecting, he went to museums and read all sorts of art-related books to nurture this new passion.
