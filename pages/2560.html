Additional Information

David Mirvish is planning to open a public Mirvish Art Gallery in Toronto to display his collection.
From 1963 through 1975, Mirvish operated a contemporary art gallery, the David Mirvish Gallery, specialising in the American abstract painters of the 1960s and 1970s
