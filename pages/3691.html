Additional Information

He bought Sidney Nolan's "Snake" from Sotheby's in 2005, paying over 2 million Australian dollars for it.
Mobile of his Curator: 0061 (0) 438 308 161.
In 2010 Walsh made a kind of bet with French artist Christian Boltanski, who agreed to have his every move filmed 24 hours a day in his Paris studio and streamed live to Walsh's MONA for a monthly stipend. The bet was that Boltanski would only live eight years - if he lived less, Walsh would have won as he would have paid les than the artwork was worth. However, as Boltanski still lives, Walsh has lost since he's (presumably) paid more than the artwork is worth.
Walsh also collects rare books, manuscripts and documents.
