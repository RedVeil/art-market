Additional Information

Although she finds it difficult to buy large-scale works, she admits that, in the end, it is down to the individual artist.
Sarah and Gerard Griffin are supporters of the Donmar Warehouse.
She helped curate 'The Birthday Party' exhibition for London's Flow Gallery's 10th Anniversary (2009) and 'The Nature of Things' exhibition at The New Art Centre, Roche Court, Wiltshire (2012).
She places a lot of importance on the artist's needs and intentions, going to great lengths to create the perfect environment for their works on their advice.
What attracted Sarah Griffin to de Waals work was its capacity to capture and create a sense of environment.
