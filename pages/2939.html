Additional Information

She is a judge for the Lower Saxony State Prize in Hanover, the competition 'Youth Association of German local cooperative banks' in Berlin and the Museum Prize of Lower Saxony Savings Bank Foundation in Hanover. She presents the North West Awards in Bremen. In addition, she is a trustee of the Carl-von-Ossietzky University in Oldenburg and sits on the advisory board of Kikuma, a children's art magazine, published by Luebbe Verlag.
She is the widow of the founder and publisher of the magazine "Stern", Henri Nannen.
