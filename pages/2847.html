Additional Information

Due to her contributions in education, health and social service in Turkey, Suna was awarded "Supreme Service Medal" by state president Süleyman Demirel in 1997.
In 1999 The London Business School chose to grant her "honorary membership" for her contributions in leadership at the Koç Holding and in the education of children in Turkey.
She is the youngest daughter of the late Turkish tycoon Vehbi Koç.
The Pera Museum (Turkish: Pera Müzesi), is a private art museum and gallery located in Istanbul, Turkey, which was established in 2005 by the "Suna and ?nan K?raç Foundation". The collection is housed in the former Bristol Hotel, which was designed by the Ottoman Greek architect Achille Manoussos.
