Additional Information

A catalogue was published for the exhibition "Russian Avant-Garde 1910-1930 - The G. Costakis Collection" with the sponsorship of the J.F. Costopoulos Foundation, a foundation established by Annie Costopoulous family. Ms. Costopoulos played an important role for that donation.
In the 1970s Annie Kostopoulou collected art with her brother Yannis Kostopoulos. Her brother donated the collection of about 5000 artworks from diferent genres to the Alpha Bank.
Not to be confused with the contemporary Greek artist Anni(e) Costopoulou.
She is a philanthropist and has donated money for different cultural causes. She donated 1 million Drachmas for the library of the Athens School of Fine Arts.
