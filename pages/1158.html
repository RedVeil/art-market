Additional Information

A generic version of the "Rain Room" was commissioned by the Frankel Foundation. The foundation also partially funded the research and the prototyping.
Maxine and Stuart Frankel donated $10 million to the University of Michigan's Museum of Art in 2004 for the creation of a new 53,000 square-foot space. This space was given the name of The Maxine and Stuart Frankel and the Frankel Family Wing.
Together with the Samuel and Jean Frankel Foundation, the Maxine and Stuart Frankel Foundation pledged to donate $10 million to the Campaign for Cranbrook in support of Cranbrook Academy of Art and Art Museum.
