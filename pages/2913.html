Additional Information

He admires works which display a certain technicality, but believe this isn't enough: he wants to feel moved by the work and his interest in big themes, such as beauty, death or madness, come from his wanting to find a certain psychological intensity in a work.
He believes that, although the act of looking at a work of art is important, it is not enough: he wants to get involved with the work, to work with it, to collect it.
