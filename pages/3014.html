Additional Information

He used to be one of two owners of a painting by Konstantinos Parthenis, that was auctioned at a Sotheby's Greek Sale and was later involved in a lawsuit because of its inauthenticity.
Mr. Palaiokrassas has curated a variety of art exhibitions, mainly with artworks from the Dimitri Daskalopoulos Collection.
Mr. Palaiokrassas is an art historian, who is now the director of the Dimitri Daskalopoulos Collection. He also gives art advice to Mr. Daskalopoulos.
