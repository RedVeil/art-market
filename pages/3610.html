Additional Information

Guenther sold around 135 pieces from his African art collection at Sotheby's in November 2000, making USD $1,491,760, to cover the costs of caring for his wife who had Alzheimers, and to pay for his grandchildrens' education. He kept some works of contemporary [and] African art though.
