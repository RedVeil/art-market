Additional Information

He received "Montblanc de la Culture Arts Patronage Award" in 2007.
Kim bought a painting "Coca Cola"(oil on canvas) of Korean artist Ko Young-hoon at his graduation Exhibition in 1975. Since then Kim supported Ko buying his art works. Now Ko is quite famous artist in Korea.
