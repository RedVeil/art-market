Additional Information

By marrying to Kong Dongmei, Chen Dongsheng, became the grandson-in-law of Mao Zedong.
Chen Dongsheng was also listed among the most powerful people of the art world in “Power 2011”, the December issue of the magazine Art + Auction published in America.
Chen Dongsheng was voted the world's ten most important patron of the arts (Power Patron) by magazine "Art Auction", among their "Global Art Annual 100 Power List".
Founded by Chen Dongsheng, China Guardian Auctions is now China's largest auction company for artworks.
In 2012, Chen Dongsheng received the "Montblanc Arts Patronage" because of his outstanding contribution to the Chinese art market and young artists.
