Additional Information

He was a lawyer until the age of forty, when he decided to become a designer and hotelier.
In 1999, Gianni Versace booked the entire Tsitouras hotel for a month. He was very pleased with Tsitouras work, and it was him who urged Mr. Tsitouras to focus on design and the hotel.
Mr. Tsitoura is a passionate collector of art and artifacts. He counts over 4,000 Santorinian artifacts, and is currently building a museum. He wants it to cover art and artifacts from the 15th century until the 20th century.
The collector is a founder of the Archive of Therian Studies and the Museum of Santorini, a founding member of the Greek Society of Cartography and the Greek Folklore Society.
