Additional Information

The Zabludowicz Collection celebrated it's 20th birthday in 2015.
The couple have different tastes, Anita goes for eclectic emerging art.
You can also check out some of the collection at https://www.instagram.com/zabludowicz_collection/
In 2014, there was a call to boycott the Zabludowicz collection over unsubstantiated arms deals.
The couple launched their artist residency in Las Vegas in November 2015.
Anita Zabludowicz was awarded an OBE for Philanthropist, for services to the arts in 2016.
The Zabludowicz's own an "art camp" in Sarvisalo, home to the family retreat–turned–art colony and sculpture park.
