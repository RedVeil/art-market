Additional Information

He founded The Warning Gallery in order to challenge the conservative Northern Irish art establishment, which tends to focus on romantic and representational art. It seeks to model itself on the Contemporary art scene of London, New York and Berlin; cities to which Northern Irish collectors travel to purchase art. With The Warning Gallery, he seeks to bring these collectors back into the local art scene.
