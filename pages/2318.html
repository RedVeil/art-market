Additional Information

He has exhibited 50 art works of the American artist Ben Shahn from his collection at Gwangju Museum of Art in 2012. After exhibition he donated all of it to Gwangju Museum of Art.
He was awarded Order of Culture in 2012.
He's a second-generation Korean-Japanese.
His parents were born in Gwangju in South Korea, so he thinks of Gwangju as his hometown too. Since 1993, he has donated 2222 art works to the Gwangju Museum of Art and 100 million won (about 90,000USD) to the Gwangju university of education.
