Additional Information

Marwan Zakhem’s private collection of contemporary West African art is displayed throughout the Kempinski Hotel Gold Coast City.
Zakhem's Gallery 1957 opened on 7th of March 2016 with an installation by Ghanaian artist Serge Attukwei Clottey titled “My Mother’s Wardrobe.”
Zakhem says he only collects pieces that he would like to have showing in his home or office. He does not collect art with the expectation to benefit from the price appreciating in the future.
Zakhem's gallery plans to commission new works from artists including Zohra Opoku, Jeremiah Quarshie and Yaw Owusu.
Marwan Zakhem has commissioned Ibrahim Mahama to create a work for one of the walls in the Kempinski Hotel Gold Coast City foyer.
