Additional Information

Although Xie Jinmu owns more than a thousand oil paintings, he is better known as an art print collector.
Xie Jinmu also likes to buy artworks from young Chinese artists to support them.
Xie Jinmu started collecting Chinese realism oil paintings in the late 1980s, influenced by a Japanese collector couple during his study in Japan.
