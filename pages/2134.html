Additional Information

Huang Liaoyuan is considered to be one of the most important promoters/ dealers for Chinese contemporary art. He is also known as the band manager of the famous Chinese rock band "Tang Dynasty" and "Secondhand Roses".
Huang is the subject of a 2015 portrait entitled "Nothing Better - Huang Liaoyuan" by Chinese painter Yao Peng.
