Additional Information

As well as co-owning 'la Peau de l'Ours' collection, he owns his own private art collection, which he regularly displays in his Espace Robert Poulin.
Founded by Robert Poulin in 1995, 'La Peau de l'Ours' art collection is co-owned by 13 collectors, each of whom invested at least 2000$ per year into it. The purpose behind the collection was to support Contemporary artists from Quebec.
He acts as the Artistic Director for 'la Peau de l'Ours' collection.
He does not see the artworks in his private collection as 'sacred' and is ready to re-sell them, if need be, especially to raise money to buy new works. He sees part of the pleasure of collecting in making artworks circulate, in order to form new art collectors and to pass on the "collecting bug" to them.
