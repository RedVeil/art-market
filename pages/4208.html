Additional Information

At the moment, she is particularly keen on the works by Jonathan Meese and Tal R.
For her, no artwork can be too loud or obnoxious.
Her parents, Rosa and Carlos M. de la Cruz, are major Contemporary art collectors in Florida. They have opened their home in Key Biscayne to the public and are planning on establishing a private museum for Miami's design district. Her mother is particularly keen on installations and performances.
