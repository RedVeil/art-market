Additional Information

He likes art which is strong, rather than sensationalist.
He sees his collecting as an 'aesthetic investment' and looks for artworks which he cannot be kept away from and which he needs to see daily.
He started collecting with the aim of purchasing Contemporary works by black artists, but has since diversified his interests.
His first major purchase was a work by the American artist James Brown, and cost around £4,000.
