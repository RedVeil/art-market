Additional Information

The Mugrabi family bought Jean-Michel Basquiat’s 1982 painting “Untitled (Yellow Tar and Feathers)” for $25.9 million at Sotheby`s on the 14th November 2013.
While putting up his Gramercy Park house for sale, it was estimated that the the art on the walls surpassed th $8 million asking price of the triplex. The place housed works by Jean-Michel Basquiat, Andy Warhol, and Pablo Picasso.
