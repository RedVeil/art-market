Additional Information

Besides artworks, Koh Seow Chuan also collects old private documents, legal papers of land ownership and transfers, maps of Singapore and the region and photographs of early Singapore.
Koh Seow Chuan received the Patron of Heritage Award in 2006, Distinguished Patron of Heritage Award in 2008, and Patron of Heritage Award again in 2009.
Singapore Art Museum named a gallery after Mr and Mrs Koh Seow Chuan in honour of their contributions in 2007.
