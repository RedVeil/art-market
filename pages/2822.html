Additional Information

Ten percent of William Zhao's 200-300 pieces of his collection is on display at his Peak residence.
William Zhao is also increasingly involved in the French art scene, advising the French Government Commission in charge of the Joint France-China Years in 2001-2002 and the president of the Pompidou Centre Jean-Jacques Aillagon for the exhibition "Alors, Chine".
William Zhao is nominated for Prix Pictet (the global award in photography and sustainability) in 2013.
