Additional Information

Dr. Sevda Elgiz is a political scientist.
In 1997, they purchased their first work by an international artist, Peter Halley.
Initially they acquired Turkish art, especially large paintings.
The Elgiz worked with neither a particular consultant nor art dealer, relying entirely on their own taste.
