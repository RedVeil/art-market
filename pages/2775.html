Additional Information

According to the art dealers, Richard M. Tsai never bargains when buying artworks. But his wife Maggie is the opposite.
Fubon Art Foundation has sponsored more than 300 artists.
Maggie Tsai was granted the "Best Art Promoter" in 2009 by Taiwan Tatler.
The Fubon Art Foundation runs regular art programs through "Very Fun Park - Contemporary Art Exhibition of Taipei", "Fubon Forums", "Art Show" (Sponsorships for young artists to take part in GEISAI art fairs) and the publication of Eastern Taipei "Art Map".
