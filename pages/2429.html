Additional Information

In 2003, Pierre Chan bought a portrait painted by Francis Bacon for US$ 3.8 million from an auction. It was a record price for Bacon at the time.
In 2006, Pierre Chan bought "Tamsui", an oil painting by Chen Chengbo for US$4.5 million at Sotheby's Auction in Hong Kong.
Pierre Chen sold Gerhard Richter’s 1994 abstract, “Wand (Wall)" at Sotheby`s for £17.4 million after a holding period of less than 5 years.
In 2015, Chen spent $26 million on acquiring Peter Doig’s painting Swamped (1990) at Christie's in New York, setting a new auction record for the artist.
Reportedly at the beginning of 2019 Chen announced he had plans to build a permanent site for his collection, although The Yageo Foundation later said that was not true.
