Additional Information

He is one of the top 200 international collectors though he keeps very private about his collection and bidding.
He was a frequent buyer at Thomas Ammann Fine Art in the 1970s and 1980s. Thomas Ammann was the leading art dealer of his generation.
It has beeen reported that Mr. Martinos bought -– for $28 million -- Eileen Gray’s Dragon armchair at the Christie’s Paris sale of Yves St. Laurent’s art collection (The second most expensive piece of furniture ever sold).
