Additional Information

Susan Bay Nimoy served on MOCA’s board for about eight years until 2006, when she resigned in frustration over the steady overspending.
Susan Bay Nimoy and her late husband, Leonard Nimoy, helped fund L.A. artist Mark Bradford’s first retrospective exhibition, in 2010 at the Wexner Center for the Arts in Colombus, Ohio
