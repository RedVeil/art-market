Additional Information

In 2012 the Burger Collection organized a keynote at Art HK which was called "The Collection as Social Sculpture?".
The Burger Collection is patron of Para/Site and AAA (Asia Art Archive) in Hong Kong, KHOJ Alternative Space in New Delhi, Hong Kong Museum of Art, C&G Artpartment, Asia Society Hong Kong Center and the Kunsthalle Zürich.
Works from the Burger Collection have been lent to museums and institutions all over the world, notably The Nasher Museum of Art in North Carolina, the Pinakothek der Moderne in Munich, the Museum of Contemporary Art in Tokyo, the Palais de Tokyo in Paris, the Tate Modern in London, the Guggenheim Museum in Bilbao and the Art Institute of Chicago.
