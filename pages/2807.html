Additional Information

Tan Zhi bought Leng Jun's "Portrait of Xiao Luo" for around US$5 million and Chen Danqing's "Lover" for US$600,000 at an auction in 2010. This is the first time Tan Zhi caught attention of the media in the field of art collecting.
Tan Zhi bought Luo Zhongli's "Child" for around US$515,000 and Lui Liu's "Strawberry Season" for US$370,000 at an auction in 2011 Autumn.
Tan Zhi bought Yang Feiyun's "Gazing" for around US$290,000 and Guo Runwen's "Marks of Time" for around US$80,000 at an auction in 2011 spring.
