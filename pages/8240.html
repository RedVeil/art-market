Additional Information

The Al Maaden Sculpture Park is the first sculpture park in Africa. It is located on an artificially made golf course near the Atlas Mountains.
Fondation Alliances' Museum of African Contemporary Art is designed by the Spanish architects Fuensanta Nieto and Enrique Sobejano.
Alami Lazraq is also interested in Chinese contemporary art.
Lazraq collects together with his son, Othman Lazraq.
