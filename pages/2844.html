Additional Information

According to Bao Mingshan, Sun Guangxin's art advisor, Sun has spent more than US$450 million buying artworks in the past 10 years.
Bao Mingshan is the aspiring director of Guangxin Xue Lian Tang Modern Art Museum.
In June 2012, Sun Guangxin invested 70 pieces of Chinese Paintings, as a qualification of asset of US$565 million into his company. These paintings will be exhibited at Guangxin Xue Lian Tang Modern Art Museum, a company owned museum planned to be built in 2013.
Sun Guangxin was listed as #27 on the 2012 Hurun China Rich List.
