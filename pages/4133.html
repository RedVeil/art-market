Business

Additional Information

He is an Irish property investor and businessman.
He is considered to be a modern day art patron.
In 2003, he bought the French château La Coste near Aix-en-Provence and developed an art and architecture project. He invited artists and architects to built sculptures and installations on his property. The project was managed by his sister Mara McKillen.
