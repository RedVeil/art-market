Additional Information

In 2012, Wang Wei and Liu Yiqian were listed in the top 200 art collectors around the world by Art News. This is the first time for the Chinese collectors to appear in this ranking.
In 2012, Wang Wei attended "Rethinking Museums | Private Museum Forum" at Art HK 12.
Wang Wei and Liu Yiqian (couple) are considered as the biggest collectors in China. Their private museum has a contemporary collection of 4000 pieces artworks, with an estimate market value of USD 650 million. The museum has 12,000 sqm exhibition space.
Wang Wei was voted Best Collector at Art Finance magazine’s Art Power awards in 2010.
