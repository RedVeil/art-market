Additional Information

"Shanghe Art Museum", founded by Chen Jiagang in 1998, has the very first corporate collection of contemporary art in China.
Chen Jiagang is believed to be the very first collector of Chinese contemporary art back in the 1980s.
In 1992, Chen Jiagang founded the Chengdu House Property Developing Company. In 1996, he founded the Sichuan Gang Jia Architecture Design Company. Chen Jiagang was considered as a very successful architect and real estate business man in the 90s until his companies went bankruft and he switched to be a independent photographer.
Since 2001, Chen Jiagang has won many awards as a photographer and his work is exhibiting around the world.
