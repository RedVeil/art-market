Additional Information

In 2011, he received the Culture Award, the Secretary of Culture of the State of Rio de Janeiro, in the category Material Heritage.
Márcio Roiter was a curator for the following exhibits: -"Art Deco - Berardo Collection, What a Wonderful World", on Madeira Island, 2010/2011, -"The Art Deco house carioca" (Cultural Peninsula, 2006/2007), -"Gallé et Rio de Janeiro" (Parc des Expositions , Nancy, 2005), -"Hommage à René Lalique" (France-Brazil House, 1992)
Márcio Roiter was the organizer of the 11th World Congress on Art Deco in ICADS (International Coalition of Art Deco Societies), held in Rio in August 2011.
