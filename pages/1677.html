Additional Information

Dani Levinas began collecting contemporary art in 1970 in his native Argentina.
The Levinas' collection started with Argentine artists and evolved when Dani and his family immigrated to the United States in 1980.
In 2015, the state of Washingtong DC cancelled his bid to transform the historic Franklin School into an arts education center, and decided to reopen the competition.
