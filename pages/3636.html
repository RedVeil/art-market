He has made it a habit to have his morning tea in the drawing room where the walls are filled with the family's favorite paintings as well as some exquisite silver-ware and art books.
Mr and Mrs Hanif says that they had landed with two fake Sadequains, bought for Rs 15,000 each many years ago. They still have the paintings, but they have been put away.
