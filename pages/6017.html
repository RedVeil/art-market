Additional Information

Joch Zeitz will open the first contemporary art museum in South Africa, The Zeitz Museum of Contemporary Art Africa (Zeitz Mocaa), which is expected to be completed by September 2017.
The artist residency programme of the Zeitz Foundation, based at the Segera Retreat in the centre of Laikipia, hosts artists dealing with cultural discourses about Africa.
Zeitz presents his art collection at is Segera Ranch in the "Zeitz Sculpture Garden".
