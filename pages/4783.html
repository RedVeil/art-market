Additional Information

Her first encounter to photography began when she was 5.
Her inspiration in photography comes from people who shoots film on the street, emotional shapes of the street and those who randomly talk to her on the street.
Her passion for contemporary art started with photography and grew very quickly as she went to the darkroom and galleries more often.
She has always been involved in art since she was growing up and it was always important in her life.
She has been to exhibition in Berlin such as BlainSouthern as well as in New York like Richard Artschwager show at the Whitney and George Bellows at the Met.
She is more keen on the historical and academic side of art.
