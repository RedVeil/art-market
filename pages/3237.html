Additional Information

He is a founder of "ShimoumArt" which organize young artists's exhibitions. He has been supporting artist exchange programmes for young artists between Copenhagen and Tokyo.
He is a freelancer curator, art consultant, translator. He worked at the German Embassy in Copenhagen as a translator, especially in art or art history related texts.
He lived in Tokyo for 3 years (2010-2012). During his stay in Tokyo, he purchased at least one artwork every month.
