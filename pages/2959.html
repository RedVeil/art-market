Additional Information

In 2010 Mr. Comninos bought an artwork by Greek artist Nikos Hadjikyriakos-Ghikas for € 520.809 at a Sotheby's auction.
Mr. Anthony Comninos started his shipping career in 1964 and was instrumental in building up a substantial business which over the years operated over 300 vessels consisting of bulk carriers, reefers, containers, multipurpose vessels and tankers.
Mr. Comninos Foundation supports culture and arts.
