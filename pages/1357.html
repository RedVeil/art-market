Additional Information

13 mainly postwar classics from the collection raised $44.3 million at a Christie's auction in 2005. Willem de Kooning’s painting Sail Cloth, 1949, was purchased by collector Sammy Ofter for $13.1 million (estimate: $8/12 million). Larry Gagosian bought Franz Kline’s Crow Dancer, 1958, for $6.4 million (estimate: $4/6 million), as well as Arshile Gorky’s Composition 11, 1943, for $2.7 million (estimate: $900,000/1.2 million). The sold paintings were donated by Jonas to the Jewish Communal Fund before the auction.
Jonas and his wife lent a Jackson Pollock painting to the Deutsche Guggenheim in Berlin for an exhibition.
