Additional Information

Peter Woods and Francis Ryan refer to themselves as 'hoarders' rather than collectors.
They have made use of the 'Own Art' scheme, which offers collectors the possibility of spreading the cost of a contemporary art purchase over 10 months. They have also negotiated with artists directly to reserve works or pay over a period of time.
They regularly attend openings, degree shows and make studio visits.
