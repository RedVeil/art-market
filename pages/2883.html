Additional Information

Although he is most famed for his Orientalist art collection, he also has collections of 19th century, contemporary photography and antique books.
Shafik Gabr bought Elisabeth Jerichau-Baumann`s "An Egyptian Pottery Seller near Gizeh" (1876) for £67,500 at Christie’s London on June 18, 1998.
