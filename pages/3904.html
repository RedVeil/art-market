Additional Information

Mrs Claudia Beck and Mr Andrew Gruft started the NOVA Gallery to show photographs by such artists as Fox Talbot, Julian Margaret, Walker Evans, Ian Wallace and N.E. Thing Co. When the gallery was closed in 1982, they donated a large portion of their photography collection to the Vancouver Art Gallery, which was marked by an exhibition titled Real Pictures.
