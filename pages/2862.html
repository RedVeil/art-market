Additional Information

He established Hiscox Art Projects, a gallery space where he encourages young British artists by allowing them to exhibit their work there.
He has had to build a warehouse on the land of his Wiltshire home to house his own art collection as it is so big.
He stated that he is happy to purchase art online and unseen from digital images sent by galleries, but that the main factor in this is that he already knows and trusts the galleries and sources he buys from in this way.
Hiscox purchases works for his company's corporate collection, and there are now over 50 different artists exhibited in their offices and spaces worldwide.
