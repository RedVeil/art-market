Additional Information

Herman Heinsbroek previously served as a diplomat in the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Netherlands, vice-president to CBS Netherlands, Belgium and Luxembourg and CEO to Arcade Music. Now he devotes most of his time in art collecting, e.g. visiting art fairs, galleries and auctions.
