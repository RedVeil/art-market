Additional Information

Michael Klaar had many cooperations with museums through his musical work. In 2004 he played Bach's "Wohltemperiertes Klavier II, Fuga VII" combined with a light installation from Thomas Baumann in the Joanneum in Graz. In 2012 he was in the jury for the "Format-Kunstguide" to evaluate the value of Austrian artists.
