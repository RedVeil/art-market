Business

Additional Information

Celebrity Cruises was created in 1989 by the Chandris Group shipping company, and quickly gained a reputation for stylish vessels, innovative spas, exceptional cuisine. Especially the Mercury line became famous for the remarkable collection of contemporary art that was shown on board, chosen by Christina Chandris from the family's collection.
Christina is part of the Greek shipping family founded by John D. Chandris in 1915.
She has organized exhibitions with works from her collection on Celebrity Cruise ships and other spaces.
She has worked on a number of charitable appeals including initiatives for the Red Cross and NSPCC’s Full Stop Appeal.
She is a fine arts curator.
The Chandris-family owns the Chandris hotel chain. The Met Hotel in Thessaloniki is their newest edition. Artworks from the family's collection cover the walls, chosen and curated by Mrs. Chandris.
