Additional Information

In 2001, Jean-Marc Decrop sold all his personal collection of Figuration Narrative to buy exclusively Chinese art.
Jean-Marc Decrop is a close friend of Guy Ullens. Ullens has purchased many artworks under the advice of Decrop.
Jean-Marc Decrop was appointed as an expert at the French National Chamber of Experts (Chambre Nationale des Experts Spécialisés en Objets d'Art et de Collection, CNES) for Chinese contemporary art.
