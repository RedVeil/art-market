Additional Information

Wang Taiqing has been travelling very frequently between mainland China and Taiwan in the past 20 years to collect Chinese art.
Wang Taiqing has made art collecting a career for himself in the past 45 years. Although keeping a low profile, Wang is known for owning a few hundred pieces of artworks from Qi Baishi and Wu Guanzhong.
