Additional Information

According to ART news magazine he is one of the top collectors worldwide in the impressionist, modern, post-war and contemprorary art fields.
He also collects Chinese ceramics.
He has been involved in collecting artworks since his childhood and known as a major art collector.
He is a member of Cingillioglu family, one of the most prominent and best known families in Turkey.
Kemal Has Cingillioglu, the son of Halit Cingillioglu, bought Cy Twombly`s "Untitled (Rome)" from 1960 for $4,413,110 at Christie`s in February 2014.
