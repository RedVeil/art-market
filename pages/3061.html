Additional Information

Chan Kok Hua's Sin Hua Gallery is the biggest gallery in Singapore dealing Chinese art.
In 2010, Chan Kok Hua donated a total of 10 artworks to the National Art Gallery, Singapore. His donation consisted of ink paintings by Singapore artists Chen Wen Hsi, Chen Chong Swee, Fan Chang Tien, Huang Pao Fang and David Kwo and calligraphy works by Pan Shou, Venerable Song Nian and Tan Swie Hian.
