Additional Information

Jayne Wrightsman was Jackie Kennedy's best friend.
She and her late husband donated David’s "Virgin and Child with Four Angels", Vermeer’s "Portrait of a Young Woman", as well as works by El Greco, Giovanni Battista Tiepolo, Georges de La Tour, Rubens and Jacques-Louis David to the Metropolitan Museum of Arts. They also sponsored the Museum’s eight Wrightsman Rooms.
