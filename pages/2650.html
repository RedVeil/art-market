Additional Information

Along with Dakis Joannou and Dinos Martinos, he is one of the most notable Greek contemporary art collectors.
He has been investing in contemporary art since 1994.
He is planning to open a permanent place to show his collection.
He was CEO of the company Delta Dairy, the biggest Greek milk and ice cream factory. He sold his shares for € 421 million in 2007.
In 2000 Daskalopoulos bought an edition of Sherrie Levine’s ‘Fountain (Buddha)’ for $48,000. The price for Levine’s fountain’s have since spiralled to $446,500 at auction.
