Additional Information

Arthur Mbanefo serves as an Executive Officer at Paloma Partners LLC. Chief (Amb.)
He established the Mbanefo Foundation to promote Nigerian art abroad. He works for the foundation with his son, who is also an art collector.
He has been a Director of Citibank Nigeria Limited since January 2009.
He is a businessman and a renowned art collector.
He serves as a Member of Business Advisory Board at LaGray Chemical Company Ltd. and LaGray Chemical Company, Inc.
He was a permanent Nigerian Representative to the UN.
Mr. Mbanefo is the Odu of Onitsha and Oluwo Adimula.
