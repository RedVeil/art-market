Additional Information

Budi Tek is in the process of creating what will be known as "Budi Desa," an art-filled village in Bali, Indonesia.
In 2017, Tek was awarded the French Legion of Honor.
Tek was diagnosed with pancreatic cancer in 2015 and has been battling against it since. His short life-expectancy is the motivation behind his rushed endeavours in the art world.
Tek hopes to turn his private Yuz Museum into a pubic institution and, as such, has brokered a landmark partnership with the Los Angeles County Museum of Art to help him progress in this mission. As of 2020, there are no real state-run contemporary art museums in China; they're all privately owned.
