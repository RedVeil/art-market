Additional Information

He has been collecting for over a decade along with his wife, Abby Binay-Campos
He often used to watch his mother, Nina Nakpil Campos, paint part of her hobby.
He owns a venue on Avenue Makati where he uses as a gallery called "Botong's" Carlos “Botong” Francisco.
He won a silver medal in an art contest in middle school and then later as a gold one representing De La Salle-Zobel.
Quite a few of his family members have strong relations to art. For example, his great-aunt was an artist and a fine jewelry designer; his maternal father was an architect who designed the National Press Club.
