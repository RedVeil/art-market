Additional Information

Rik Reinking begin acquiring artworks at age 16.
In 2008, Rik Reinking bought a work by Wim Delvoye entitled "TIM" for 150,000 euros (£130,000). The controversy is that this work is a tattoo on the back of a living man named Tim Steiner. When Steiner dies, his back will be skinned and the skin framed permanently, taking up a place in Reinking's personal art collection.
