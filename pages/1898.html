Additional Information

Rudy Tseng has curated two exhibitions: "Art of Life: Contemporary Paintings and Sculptures in Taiwan and Japan" (2010), "Documented, Doubted, and Imagined Realities : Contemporary Photography from Japan and Taiwan" (2011) and " Discreet Charm of The Bourgeoisie: Contemporary Visions on China" (2012).
Rudy Tseng regularly writes for a column at Art and Investment Magazine.
Rudy Tseng was a former CEO for The Walt Disney Company (Taiwan).
Rudy Tseng was teaching as a assistant professor at Shih Chien University for 3 semesters.
