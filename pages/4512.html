Additional Information

Apart from collecting art works, he also collects maps, especially ancient maps.
He believes that collecting art is a mission rather than a hobby.
He has built up his collection by going a lot of to exhibitions to update himself with the latest art works and also to spot new talents.
He has lent some of the paintings from his own collection to the Ateneo Art Gallery. Also, he has given some of them to his relatives or other collectors and has been helping friends to start their own collections.
He has written 5 books related to Philosophy.
More than 70 of his works were at the auction at the Finale Art File in October 2011.
Not only does he collects art works but he also shares them.
When collecting, price is not an obstacle to him.
