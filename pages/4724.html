Additional Information

Her favourite piece is by Fabian de la Rosa of a woman on the dressing table.
She paints as a hobby and her art passion has been influenced very much by her mother, Pura Villanueva-Kalaw.
She used her income gained from working in real estate to support young, struggling artists.
