Additional Information

Although the loan of his collection to the Irish Writers' Centre became a permanent donation due to his illness, his recovery prompted him to resume his collecting again.
He enjoys the various aspects of the collecting experience: from the searching and finding; to the acquiring and the installation, and finally to the study of the work and to sharing it with others.
He now mostly collects works by recent graduates, stating that they are the only artworks he can now afford to purchase.
The first painting he purchased was a work by the artist Patrick Hickey, 'Still Life in Purple', for £90.
