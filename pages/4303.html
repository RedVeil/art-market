Additional Information

As an art consultant at DCA, he helps his customers try and build up their own collection, reflecting on their individuality and personality.
He believes that investing into art pieces is a sound investment and so at the same time, he invests into stock markets parallel to his art investment.
He helped Gridthiya's non-profit art space Project 304 financially.
He is willing to show his private collection and people can make an appointment by calling (087) 597 3340.
He often visits galleries and bids at auctions.
He reads books on arts and is impressed by artists such as Leonardo Da Vinci and Michelangelo.
He started collecting when he was about 20 years old and has been collecting for 15 years now.
He values the art pieces not by the the price, but by the time and result and the value the art piece means to him.
When younger, he was only able to afford small art works.
