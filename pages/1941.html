Additional Information

In 2005, Su Minluo spent US$ 346,013 on a painting by a contemporary Chinese artist Wu Guanzhong during an auction by Hanhai Auction Company. Later the painting was identified fake by the artist himself. But Su Minluo lost the law case according to the current Chinese Auction Law.
